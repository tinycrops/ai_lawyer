(a)

The service of an order or notice for the correction of violations of this article shall be made upon the owner, occupant or other person responsible for the conditions, either by personally delivering a copy of such order or notice to such person or by delivering such order or notice to and leaving it with any person in charge of the premises or by sending a copy of the order or notice by certified or registered mail to the owner's last known address.

(b)

When buildings or other premises are occupied by one other than the owner under a lease or other agreement, the order or notice issued to correct violations of this article shall apply to the occupant thereof, provided that where the order or notice requires the making of additions to or changes in the premises themselves which may become part of the real property of the owner, the order or notice shall also be issued to the owner of the premises or real property. Failure to deliver an order or notice to the owner, if other than the occupant, shall not invalidate such order or notice.
