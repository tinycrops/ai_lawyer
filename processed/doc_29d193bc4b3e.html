The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Alcoholic beverage means any beer, malt, wine, spirit, liquor or other beverage whose sale is regulated and licensed by the state alcoholic beverage control board.

Public recreation area means all public areas designated or approved for public use by federal, state or local governmental agencies for access to the Broad River located in the unincorporated portions of the county, including public access areas, public recreation areas, and motor vehicle parking areas.
