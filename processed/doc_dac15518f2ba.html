An officer or employee connected with the building inspection department, except one whose only connection is as a member of the board of adjustment established by this article, shall not be financially interested in the furnishing of labor, materials, or appliances for the construction, alteration, or maintenance of a building or in the making of plans specifications therefore, unless he is the owner of such building. Such officer or employee shall not engage in any work, which is inconsistent with his duties or with the interest of the department.
