Dispute resolution procedures are available for all building construction and repair projects under this article. The dispute resolution procedures are available to all parties involved in the construction project, including the architect, the construction manager and the contractors, including all levels of subcontractors, and it must be available for any issue arising out of the contract or construction process so long as the matter in dispute is $15,000.00 or more. See the county's dispute resolution policy.

(Ord. No. 2015-03, 3-16-2015)
