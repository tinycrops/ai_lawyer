(a)

A quorum of the planning board shall consist of a majority of the board membership (excluding vacant seats). A quorum is necessary for the board to take official action.

(b)

All actions of the planning board shall be taken by majority vote, a quorum being present.

(c)

A roll call vote shall be taken upon the request of any member.
