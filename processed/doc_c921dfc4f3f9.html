(a)

The final assessment resolution passed pursuant to this article may not differ in scope or reflect a cost increase from the preliminary assessment resolution. If, after the public hearing and other due consideration, the board determines a change should be made, there must be a new publication and hearing upon a new preliminary assessment resolution and assessment roll.

(b)

If the board of commissioners decides a different percentage of the cost should be assessed than proposed in the preliminary assessment resolution or the project should be greater in scope than described in the original resolution, it shall adopt a new preliminary assessment resolution and advertise such resolution as provided in this article.
