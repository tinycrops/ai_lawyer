It shall be the policy of the county to provide for the orderly and adequate development of water and/or sewer systems within the county. In keeping with this policy, the county shall review the applications for permits for construction or expansion, and shall continuously review the operation thereof, in light of the county's overall plan of development, tie-in prospects with regional development, future needs as to line size and location, the applicant's plans for expansion of the area served and any other valid considerations the county deems appropriate, which include, but are not limited to, regulations and requirements of all state and federal agencies.
