This division establishes the regulations for extension of water service in the county and applies to all county-owned revenue sharing and revolving loan program lines. The county will extend water infrastructure according to the terms and conditions in this division.

(Ord. No. 2006-07, 6-15-2006; Ord. No. 2017-11, 9-18-2017; Ord. No. 2020-17, 10-19-2020)
