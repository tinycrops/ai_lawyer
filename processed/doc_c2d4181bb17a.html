All work done under this article shall be warranted for no less than one year. All manufacturing and material warranties shall be submitted to the director of facilities or director of utilities and engineering prior to semifinal inspection. All wastewater, sewer, potable water and building design as-built drawings shall be submitted to the director of facilities of the director of utilities and engineering within 120 days following project completion.

(Ord. No. 2015-03, 3-16-2015)
