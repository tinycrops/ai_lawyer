A franchise to operate an ambulance service may provide:

(1)

The number of ambulances that shall be operated within the area of the franchise.

(2)

A schedule of rates, fees, and charges that may be charged, which rates, fees and charges may be revised from time to time.

(3)

The minimum limits of liability insurance coverage.

(4)

Such other regulations consistent with and supplementary to any statute or any department of human resources regulation relating to ambulance services.
