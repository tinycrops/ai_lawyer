There is hereby created an animal control department which shall be a division of the sheriff's department, and shall be composed of such employees as shall be determined by the county commissioners. Such employees shall be appointed by the sheriff pursuant to G.S. 153A-103 and compensated in accordance with the personnel policies of the county.

Appeals provided for in this chapter shall be to the sheriff.

The sheriff is specifically appointed as the animal cruelty investigator for the county pursuant to G.S. 19A-45.
