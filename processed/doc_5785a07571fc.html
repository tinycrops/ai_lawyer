Temporary wireless communications facilities may be permitted by administrative approval for a term not to exceed 30 days. Once granted, a temporary wireless communications facility permit may be extended for an additional 30 days upon evidence of need by the applicant. In case of emergency (e.g., storm damage to an existing tower or other circumstances resulting in the interruption of existing service) the administrative review shall be expedited to the extent feasible.
