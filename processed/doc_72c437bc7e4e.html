Installation of water and sewer facilities shall be in compliance with the requirements of state and federal law and agency regulations, including, but not limited to, the state building code, the state plumbing code, requirements of the division of health services and the office of water and air resources. In the event of a conflict between this article and such requirements, the more stringent shall apply.
