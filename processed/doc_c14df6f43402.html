(a)

Imposed. A monthly charge is hereby imposed upon each exchange access facility subscribed to by telephone subscribers whose exchange access lines are in the area served or which would be served by the county enhanced 9-1-1 service. The monthly charge shall be uniform and shall be charged to each exchange access facility regardless of the type of the exchange access facility used. The amount of such charge shall be as set from time to time by the board of commissioners, and a schedule of such charges shall be kept on file in the county clerk's office.

(b)

Annual adjustment. Prior to each fiscal year of the county, along with and at the same time as a public hearing conducted for the purpose of adopting the budget for the following fiscal year, the board of commissioners shall determine the amount of the monthly 9-1-1 charge for the following fiscal year.
