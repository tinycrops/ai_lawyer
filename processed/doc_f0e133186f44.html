The purpose of this article is to:

(1)

Regulate the location, operation and care of hazardous waste and/or low-level radioactive waste management facilities dealing with the storage, treatment or disposal of hazardous waste and/or low-level radioactive waste within the county.

(2)

Ensure that the best management practices are used in handling such hazardous waste and/or low-level radioactive waste.

(3)

Ensure that before such hazardous waste and/or low-level radioactive waste is placed into permanent or long-term storage, the best available technology is used in treating such waste, including reuse, recycling, neutralization, detoxification, incineration and maximum volume reduction. When these alternatives are not technologically feasible, retrievable above-ground storage (or below-ground if such hazardous wastes and/or low-level radioactive wastes are explosive or flammable) is preferable to other means of disposal until appropriate methods of recycling or detoxification of the stored hazardous wastes and/or low-level radioactive wastes are found, as directed by the N.C. Waste Management Act, 1981 N.C. Sessions Laws, Chapter 704.
