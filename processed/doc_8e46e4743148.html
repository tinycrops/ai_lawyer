Any owner who has received an order to take corrective action may appeal from the order to the board of commissioners by giving notice of appeal in writing to the administrator and the clerk within ten days following issuance of the final order. In the absence of an appeal, the order of the administrator shall be final. The board of commissioners shall hear an appeal within a reasonable time and may affirm, modify and affirm, or revoke the order.
