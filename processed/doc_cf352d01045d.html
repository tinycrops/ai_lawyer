(a)

It is the purpose of this section to provide for the health, safety and welfare of the residents of the county and the safe, unimpeded, normal and orderly flow of traffic on certain heavily trafficked areas of streets, highways and roadways within the county, through the proper regulation of pedestrians in and around such rights-of-way.

(b)

Standing, sitting, or loitering on any street, highway, median or right-of-way of a multi-lane divided highway or where traffic is controlled by a traffic control device is prohibited. It shall be unlawful for any person to stand, sit, or loiter, in or on any street or highway, including the shoulders or median strip, or right-of-way, but excluding sidewalks, on a street or highway where such is a multi-lane divided street or highway or where traffic on such street or highway is controlled by a traffic control device, for the purpose of soliciting from or distributing items to, the driver or occupants of any vehicle.

(c)

Any person found to be in violation of this section shall be guilty of a class 3 misdemeanor and can be fined or imprisoned in accordance with section 1-14 of the Catawba County Code. Each day any violation of this section shall continue shall constitute a separate offense.

(Ord. No. 2010-03, 4-5-2010; Ord. No. 2022-02, 1-18-2022)
