Any person, including an individual, owner, occupant, tenant, lessee, firm or business entity, and including any hauler, franchised collector, or other collector or provider, who violates the provisions of this article or any local or state health or solid waste rules or regulations adopted pursuant to this article shall be guilty of a Class 3 misdemeanor punishable by a fine of up to $500.00 or a maximum 30 day's imprisonment as provided in G.S. 14-4 or be subject to a civil penalty in the amount of $50.00 for the first violation, and a civil penalty of $100.00 for a second or subsequent violation.
