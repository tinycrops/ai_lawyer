The board of commissioners of the county, pursuant to the authority conferred by an act of the General Assembly of the state (G.S. ch. 153A, art. 18, pt. 2 (G.S. 153A-330 et seq.)), does hereby ordain and enact into law these articles and sections. The board of commissioners also repeals article 200, subdivision regulations, in the water supply watershed management and protection ordinance adopted January 1, 1994. It is not the intent to fully repeal the water supply watershed management and protection ordinance, only article 200.

(Ord. of 9-15-1998, app. B, art. II)
