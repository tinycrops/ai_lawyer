It shall be unlawful for any person to interfere with, hinder or molest an employee of the animal services division or any other person duly authorized by this chapter while in the performance of any duty as set out in this chapter. It shall also be unlawful for any person to seek to release, attempt to release, or to release any animal in the custody of the animal services division, except as otherwise specifically provided in this chapter. A violation of this section shall be punishable as a class III misdemeanor.
