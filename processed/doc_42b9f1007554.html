The counties of Catawba, Iredell, Lincoln and Mecklenburg are authorized by joint resolution to create the Lake Norman Marine Commission. The commission shall enjoy the powers and have the duties and responsibilities conferred upon it by joint resolution, subject to this article. The provisions of any joint resolution may be modified, amended or rescinded by a subsequent joint resolution. A county may unilaterally withdraw from participation as required by any joint resolution or this article, once the commission has been created, and any county may unilaterally withdraw from the commission at the end of any budget period. Upon the effectuation of such withdrawal, the commission is dissolved until reestablished under this article or any successor act of the state, and all property of the commission shall be distributed to or divided among the four counties and any other public agency serving the Lake Norman area in a manner deemed equitable by the four counties.
