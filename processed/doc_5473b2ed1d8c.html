The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Board means the board of commissioners of one of the four counties.

Commission means the Lake Norman Marine Commission or its governing board, as the case may be.

Commissioner means a member of the governing board of the commission.

Four counties means Catawba, Iredell, Lincoln and Mecklenburg counties.

Joint resolution means a resolution substantially identical in content adopted separately by the board in each of the four counties.

Lake Norman means the impounded body of water along the Catawba River in the four counties extending from the bridge crossing of Rural Road 1004 downstream to Cowans Ford Dam.

Shoreline area, except as modified by a joint resolution of the four counties, means the area within the four counties lying within one mile of the mean high water line of Lake Norman. In addition, the shoreline area shall include all islands within Lake Norman and all peninsulas extending into the waters of Lake Norman.

Wildlife commission means the state wildlife resources commission.
