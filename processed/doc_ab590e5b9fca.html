The Morganton affiliate of the Burke County Public Library is owned by the county and the City of Morganton. The Valdese affiliate of the Burke County Public Library is owned by the Town of Valdese and the county. The Hildebran affiliate of the Burke County Public Library is owned by the Town of Hildebran and the county. The library director, with the approval of the county manager, may make the necessary arrangements for those two facilities to continue to be operated as a part of the Burke County Library Public System by securing long-term leases or making such other arrangements as may be necessary.
