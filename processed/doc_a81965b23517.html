(a)

If an animal dies while under observation for rabies, the head of such animal shall be immediately submitted to the county health department for shipment to the laboratory section of the department of environment, health, and natural resources for rabies diagnosis.

(b)

The carcass of any animal suspected of dying of rabies shall be surrendered to the animal control department. The head of such animal shall be immediately submitted to the county health department for shipment to the laboratory section of the department of environment, health, and natural resources for rabies diagnosis.

(c)

A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
