Neither the applicant, the owner, the operator or any other individual whose name is required to be disclosed on the application, if different from the applicant, shall:

(1)

Engage in prostitution or suffer or permit any employee or other person on the licensed premises to engage in prostitution;

(2)

Possess, sell or use any controlled substance or suffer or permit any employee or other person on the licensed premises to possess, sell or use any controlled substance, as those terms are defined by the General Statutes of North Carolina; or

(3)

Appear nude in violation of the terms of this article, or suffer or permit any employee or other person on the licensed premises to be nude in violation of this article.
