The county zoning administrator shall present the estimated costs of the street improvement to the board of commissioners. The costs will include all work necessary to complete the paving project, including but not limited to the actual amount to be paid to the department of transportation, but shall include preliminary costs of surveying, when necessary, and attorney's fees. The board of commissioners shall determine the project's total cost when the project is completed.
