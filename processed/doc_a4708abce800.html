(a)

Composition; terms. The commission shall consist of 12 members. All members of the commission shall reside within the city or county. In addition, the members of the resource commission shall have demonstrated special interest, experience or education in history, archaeology or architecture. The terms of the commissioners shall be three years, with replacements to be appointed at the rate of four commissioners per year.

(b)

Appointment. Initially, the commissioners shall be appointed for staggered terms in the following manner: six members shall be appointed by the city council and six by the county board of commissioners.

(c)

Replacement. For replacement appointments two commissioners will be appointed by the city council and two commissioners by the board of commissioners.

(d)

Additional terms. The members of the resources commission shall serve at the pleasure of the appointing governing body. Commissioners may be reappointed for additional terms at the discretion of the appointing governing body.
