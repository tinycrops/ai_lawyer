The following guidelines shall apply to the installation of all new fire hydrants and the replacement of existing hydrants already in service:

(1)

All hydrants shall be installed in accordance with the water utility provider's standards and specifications.

(2)

Hydrants shall be located as close as possible to street intersections or areas of direct vehicular access. Larger industrial commercial or multifamily developments may require additional hydrants to compensate for long hose lays and/or greater water demand.

(3)

Hydrants should be located close to street access to facilitate easy hookup.

(4)

Hydrants located in areas designated for parking shall be afforded some type of protection from collision of vehicular traffic.

(5)

Hydrants shall be located at least four feet from any solid object, such as a power pole, tree, building, and dumpster that may hinder access and use of the hydrant.

(Ord. No. 2006-07, 6-15-2006; Ord. No. 2017-11, 9-18-2017; Ord. No. 2020-17, 10-19-2020)
