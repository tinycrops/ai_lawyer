(a)

It shall be unlawful for any person to loiter in or upon the public grounds or public buildings belonging to the county and/or those buildings occupied by branches of government of the county between the hours of 7:00 p.m. and 7:00 a.m., except as follows:

(1)

It shall not be unlawful for employees of the county and/or state to be on the grounds during such hours.

(2)

It shall not be unlawful for individuals having business with employees of the county and/or state or agencies of the county and/or state, located on such premises, to be on the grounds during such hours when the individuals are in the company of employees of the county and/or state or are meeting employees of the county and/or state upon the grounds for specific business purposes.

(3)

It shall be permissible for individuals to be on or about the premises as specified between such hours when specific permission for ingress or egress is given in written form by the county manager for specific purposes only.

(b)

Violation of this section shall be a misdemeanor offense and be punishable as provided in section 1-14 of this Code.
