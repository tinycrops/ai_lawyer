(a)

The agreement shall provide the development uses permitted on the property, including population densities, building types, intensities, placement on the site, and design.

(b)

Said development uses permitted shall comply with all local, state, and federal requirements, except as permitted by an approved variance, conditional use permit, or parallel conditional rezoning.

(c)

Outside county land use jurisdiction, said development uses permitted shall be consistent with the adopted policies and goals of the county applicable to the scale, type, and impact of the proposed development. The commission may vary from previously adopted policies and goals upon the provision of a written explanation of said variance.
