Tax imposed. The expansion of the Local Government Sales and Use Tax Act, as defined in section 4 of chapter 832 of the 1987 Session Laws, is adopted. The taxes imposed and levied shall apply to the same extent and be subject to the same limitations as are set forth in G.S. 105-463â€”105-504.

(b)

Collection. Collection of the expanded local government sales and use tax by the state secretary of revenue and liability therefor shall begin and continue on and after March 1, 1988.

(c)

Disposition of proceeds. The net proceeds of the taxes levied in subsection (a) of this section shall be distributed by the state secretary of revenue to the county and the municipalities therein as prescribed by G.S. 105-472, 105-486, 105-493 and 105-501. The amount distributed to the county shall be divided among the county and the municipalities therein in accordance with the method previously selected by the board of commissioners in accordance with the provisions of G.S. 105-472.
