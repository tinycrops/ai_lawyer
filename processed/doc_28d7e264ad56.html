(a)

Decisions by the planning administrator. Decisions of the planning administrator relating to the administrative enforcement of the provisions herein are subject to an appeal to the board of adjustment by any person aggrieved or by an officer, department, or agency of the county affected by said decisions.

(1)

An appeal shall be submitted in writing to the planning administrator who shall refer the written appeal to the board of adjustment at the next regularly scheduled meeting at least 21 days from submission of the appeal.

(2)

Each appeal shall be accompanied by payment of a $75.00 nonrefundable fee to help defray the cost of publicizing and conducting the public hearing.

(3)

The board of adjustment may reverse or affirm, in whole or in part, or may modify the order, requirement, decision, or determination appealed from, and shall make any order, requirement, decision, or determination that in its opinion ought to be made in the circumstances. To this end, the board of adjustment has all the powers of the planning administrator from whom the appeal is taken.

(b)

Decisions by the board of adjustment. Any person that is aggrieved by the decision of the board of adjustment may, within 30 days of the board of adjustment's decision, petition the board of commissioners to review the board of adjustment's decision. The board of commissioners may uphold the action of the board of adjustment or make any other decision with reference to the variance that it deems appropriate.

(c)

Decisions by the board of commissioners. All decisions by the board of commissioners are subject to an appeal to the superior court by any person, firm, corporation, or government agency aggrieved by said decision or others.
