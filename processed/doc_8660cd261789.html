(a)

All septage management firms that discharge septage within the boundaries of the county shall discharge into one of the designated dumping stations of the City of Hickory or City of Newton during specified hours. Subject to the cities' sewer ordinances, industrial or commercial septage is not allowed to be dumped into the designated city dumping stations. No further septage land application sites shall be allowed in the county.

(b)

The use of a septage detention system is prohibited.

(c)

All septage discharged into the designated city dumping stations shall be taken only from domestic sources.
