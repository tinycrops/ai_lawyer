(a)

Cat colonies should be registered with the animal services.

(b)

Each colony must have a contact person.

(c)

Each colony must have permission from the property owner to house the colony on the premises.

(d)

Each contact person will be required to maintain a current and up-to-date log of vaccines and records of all cats in the colony.

(Ord. No. 2020-04 , 8-18-2020)
