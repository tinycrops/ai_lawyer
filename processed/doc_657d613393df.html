(a)

If an animal has a current rabies inoculation and is bitten by a known rabid animal, the animal bitten shall be revaccinated and returned to the owner.

(b)

If an animal does not have a current rabies inoculation and is bitten by a known rabid animal, the owner of the bitten animal must strictly isolate the bitten animal in an animal shelter, boarding kennel, or at a veterinary hospital for a period of six months or the animal will be immediately destroyed.

(c)

A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
