(a)

Criminal. Any person who shall violate any provision of this article shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and upon conviction shall be punished as prescribed by G.S. 14-4. Each day that any person shall continue to do any act in violation of this article shall be and constitute a distinct and separate offense.

(b)

Civil. Any person who shall engage in the business, trade or profession of massagist, or the operation of a massage parlor, or provide the services of such, without obtaining the license required by this article, shall also be subject to a civil penalty of $1,500.00, which shall be assessed by the board of commissioners upon proof of such activity. Such penalty may be recovered, if not paid within 30 days of notice of assessment, by a civil action in the nature of debt as prescribed in G.S. 153A-123(c).
