It shall be the duty of all persons holding a license under this article to file with the sheriff of the county the names of all employees of the licensee and their home addresses, home telephone numbers and places of employment. Changes in the list of employees, with the names of new employees, must be filed with the sheriff within seven (7) days from the date of any such change.
