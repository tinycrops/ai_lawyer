A monthly charge is imposed upon each exchange access facility subscribed to by telephone subscribers whose exchange access lines are in the area served or which would be served by the county enhanced 911 service. The monthly charge shall be uniform and shall be charged to each exchange access facility regardless of the type of exchange access facility used. The amount of the monthly charge shall be found on a schedule which is on file and available in the county offices.
