(a)

It shall be unlawful to vary, change, or deviate from the terms of the application, the plans and specifications, or the permit, except if the change or deviation is clearly permissible under the International Building Code (including North Carolina amendments), it may not be made until specific written approval of the proposed change or deviation has been obtained from the public safety services department.

(b)

It shall be unlawful to proceed with work on a project after a building, plumbing, heating, insulation, cooling, fire sprinkler, or electrical contractor has been released or dismissed from the job without first obtaining a letter of release from the structure's or building's owner and/or the contractor.

(c)

A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
