An appointed member of the commission who misses more than three consecutive regular meetings of the commission loses his status as a member of the commission until reappointed or replaced by the board of commissioners. Absences due to sickness, death or other emergencies of like nature shall be regarded as approved absences and shall not affect the member's status on the commission, except in case of a long illness or other cause for prolonged absence, the member may be replaced.
