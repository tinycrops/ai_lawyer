(a)

It shall be the policy of the county to review all requests for extensions of water and/or sewer mains, and, where feasible and appropriate, to make such extensions.

(b)

It shall be the policy of the county to give written approval upon proper application for the extension of water and/or sewer mains. The county may agree to bear all, part or none of the costs of such permitted extensions.

(c)

All extensions to water and/or sewer mains shall, upon the completion thereof, become the sole property of the county.

(d)

Construction of all extensions and/or sewer mains shall be in compliance with the provisions of this article.
