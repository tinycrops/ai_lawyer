No person owning or controlling real estate or other premises who voluntarily and without compensation grants the county the right to inspect, designate and use the whole or any part of such real estate or premises for the purpose of sheltering persons during an actual, impending or practice disaster situation shall be civilly liable for the death of or injury to any person on or about such real estate or premises under such license, privilege or other permission, or for loss of or damage to the property of such person.
