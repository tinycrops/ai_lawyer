(a)

The authority of the board of commissioners to finance the local share of the costs of improving subdivision and residential streets is discretionary. Before the county may finance improvements, it must receive a petition for the improvements, signed by at least 75 percent of the property owners representing 75 percent of the lineal front footage affected. There shall be a filing fee as set by the board of commissioners to cover the costs of advertising the required public hearings, certified mailings, photocopying and long distance telephone calls.

(b)

The petition must have the full names, addresses, and telephone numbers of property owners petitioning. All owners of property petitioned must sign, including wives if under joint ownership.

(c)

The petition shall be filed in the office of the zoning administrator. Upon receipt of the petition, the zoning administrator will verify the lineal front footages, the signed owners of property, and the availability of county funds.
