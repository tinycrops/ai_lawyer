Within the annual budget of the Asheville-Buncombe Community Relations Council, as may be appropriated by the board of county commissioners and the Asheville city council, from time to time, the Asheville-Buncombe Community Relations Council, in the name of fair housing commission, is authorized to:

(1)

Employ such staff as it may require.

(2)

Contract with the Asheville-Buncombe Community Relations Council for such support services as it may require.

(3)

Contract with such other third parties as the fair housing commission may deem necessary.
