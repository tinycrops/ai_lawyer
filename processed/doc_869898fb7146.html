(a)

Use of roads. It shall be unlawful for any person to operate any motorized vehicle or other power driven vehicle upon the grass or footways of the county parks or recreational facility, other than on the public roads and streets.

(b)

Maximum speed. It shall be unlawful to operate any motor vehicle in excess of 15 miles per hour upon any of the streets or drives of any county park or recreational facility park.

(c)

Parking regulations. It shall be unlawful for any person to park any motor vehicle in an area designated by no parking signs. The director of parks and recreation shall determine the areas where parking is prohibited.

(d)

Vendors. It shall be unlawful for any vehicle to park on any of the streets within any of the public parks or play areas of the county for the purpose of vending goods, wares or merchandise without prior written approval by the director of parks and recreation.
