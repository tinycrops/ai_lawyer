The selling or public distribution of drinks, food and the like must be authorized in writing by the parks and recreational director or designee. In some cases, authorization may be given through an appropriate contract. Also, authorization may be given only after review of health considerations, insurance, and after considering the appropriateness of the requested activity considering the time, place and circumstances. It is the intent of this article not to discourage church, civic groups and nonprofits from utilizing county property when appropriate.
