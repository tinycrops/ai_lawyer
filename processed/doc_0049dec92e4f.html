(a)

It shall be unlawful for an animal owner or other person to fail to comply with the state laws relating to the control of rabies.

(b)

The purpose of this article is to supplement the state laws by providing a procedure for the enforcement of state laws relating to rabies control, in addition to the criminal penalties provided by state law.

(Ord. of 8-17-2009; Ord. No. 2014-04, 6-16-2014; Ord. No. 2023-02, 3-6-2023)
