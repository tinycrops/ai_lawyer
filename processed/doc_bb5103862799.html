(a)

Required; scope. Any individual, organization, corporation, association, society, fraternity, club, order or group of whatever kind desiring the use of the public parks owned or controlled by the county as the location for a rally, demonstration, march, public speech, public meeting or other similar activity shall make written request therefor not less than 72 hours prior to the date of the intended use. This section shall apply only to assemblages where the expected attendance will be more than 100 people. No such assemblages may be held except during daylight hours.

(b)

Application; information. The application shall be submitted to the director of parks and recreation and shall state the name and address of its principal officer and of its directors or other governing body and shall also contain such other pertinent information as may be required by the director of parks and recreation in order to clearly identify the organization submitting the request and the individuals principally engaged in the conduct of its affairs. The request shall state the date and hour for which the use is requested and the expected duration of such use.

(c)

Determination by director of parks and recreation. If the director of parks and recreation shall conclude that the requested use will not hinder or impede any regularly established use of the parks and will not adversely affect their care, protection or maintenance or create a nuisance by such use, the director of parks and recreation may grant permission for such use. If the director of parks and recreation shall determine otherwise, the request shall be denied.
