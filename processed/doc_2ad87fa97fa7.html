(a)

There is hereby adopted by reference and incorporated in this article that certain code known as and titled the North Carolina State Building Code, Volume V, Fire Prevention, 1991 edition. Copies of the county fire prevention and protection code and all technical codes and standards adopted by reference shall be filed with and available for public inspection in the offices of the clerk to the board of commissioners and the fire marshal.

(b)

Amendments to codes and standards adopted by reference in this section which are adopted and published by the state building code council shall be effective in the county at the time such amendments become a part of North Carolina State Building Code, Volume V, Fire Prevention, of the state building code.
