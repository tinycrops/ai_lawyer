As provided by G.S. 160D-1203(7), the cost of any repairs, alterations or improvements, or of vacating and closing, or removal or demolition and removal, caused to be made or done by the chief building inspector pursuant to section 14-100 shall be a lien against the real property upon which such cost was incurred. Such lien shall be filed, have priority, and be collected in the same manner as the lien for special assessments established by G.S. 160A-216 et seq.
