(a)

Any person, firm or corporation who violates any provision of this article shall be subject to all penalties authorized by this article and any other applicable local, state or federal law.

(b)

A violation of any provision of this chapter for which a specific penalty has not been otherwise provided shall constitute a misdemeanor and be punishable as provided in section 1-14 of this Code.
