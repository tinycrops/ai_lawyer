(a)

Every person, corporation or partnership licensed under this article as an adult establishment shall post such license in a conspicuous place and manner on the adult establishment premises.

(b)

Every person holding a license as an entertainer shall post his or her license in his or her work area on the adult establishment premises so it shall be readily available for inspection by the county authorities responsible for enforcement of this article.
