No employee of the animal services department shall have the authority to enforce this chapter within any other corporate limits or jurisdictions, unless an interlocal agreement pursuant to G.S. 160A-460 et seq. is executed by the county and the incorporated township. Such an interlocal agreement may be entered into at any time, provided the appropriate resolution is adopted by the governing body. Any such municipality or township shall agree that this chapter, or the applicable ordinances related to animal control within the jurisdiction, shall be enforced by the animal services director within the corporate limits or jurisdiction of the municipality. This section 3-5 shall not apply to powers that the county is authorized by statute to exercise throughout incorporated and unincorporated areas of the county.
