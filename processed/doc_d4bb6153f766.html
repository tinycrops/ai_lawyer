It shall be unlawful for any person, except persons directly involved in the use of radiation machines or radioactive materials for medical diagnosis or treatment, to use, manufacture, produce, process, store, dump, bury, transport, transfer, receive, acquire, own or possess radioactive material in the county. A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
