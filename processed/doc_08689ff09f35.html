(a)

Abandoned motor vehicles unlawful. It shall be unlawful for the registered owner or person entitled to possession of a motor vehicle and for the owner, lessee, or occupant of the real property upon which the vehicle is located to leave, allow, or suffer the vehicle to remain on the property after it has been declared an abandoned motor vehicle.

(b)

Removal of abandoned motor vehicles authorized. Upon investigation, proper authorizing officials of the county may determine that a vehicle is an abandoned vehicle and order the vehicle removed.
