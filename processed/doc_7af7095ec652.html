Violation of section 50-32 shall be a class 3 misdemeanor and shall be punishable by a fine of up to $500.00 and/or incarceration for a period of time as outlined in G.S. 15A-1340.20 et seq. Each and every entrance into a park or recreation facility, regardless of the time period between such entries, shall constitute a separate offense under this article.
