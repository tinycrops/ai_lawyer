All transaction receipts or tickets shall require the following information in order to be in compliance with this article:

(1)

Full name of person making transaction (seller or client).

(2)

Address of person making transaction (seller or client).

(3)

Date of transaction, sale or exchange.

(4)

Weight of the metals purchased or sold under this article.

(5)

The amount paid for transaction.

(6)

An invoice with company name, address, phone number and invoice number.

(7)

Description of item, type of metal and grade, numbers or engravings.

(8)

Driver's license or ID card number of the seller or client.

(9)

Signatures of both seller/client and buyer or clerk employed by said business.
