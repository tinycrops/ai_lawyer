(a)

All boards and commissions of the county, if not specifically provided for within their authorizing ordinance provisions, shall elect one chairperson and one vice-chairperson. The clerk to the board of commissioners shall be notified of all board and commission officers.

(b)

All board and commission officers shall be elected for a one-year term. Such officers may be reelected for not more than three consecutive terms.
