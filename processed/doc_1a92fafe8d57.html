(a)

The board of adjustment shall establish a regular meeting schedule and shall meet frequently enough so that it can take action in conformity with section 8-51 (applications to the process expeditiously).

(b)

The board shall conduct its meetings in accordance with the quasi-judicial procedures set forth in sections 4, 6 and 7 [of this ordinance].

(c)

All meetings of the board shall be open to the public, and whenever feasible the agenda for each board meeting shall be made available in advance of the meeting.
