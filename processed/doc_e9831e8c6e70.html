The intent and purpose of this article is to establish the duties of the county emergency management agency as it relates to hazardous materials emergencies. Such incidents include, but are not limited to, spills, accidents, illegal dumping and other releases or threatened releases of hazardous materials requiring control. The emergency management agency shall have the authority to summarily remove, abate, or remedy hazardous material emergencies within the jurisdiction of the county that are, or potentially are, a threat to public safety.

(Ord. No. 2013-10, 8-5-2013)
