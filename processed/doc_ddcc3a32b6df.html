(a)

A person shall not camp in a county park or recreation area except:

(1)

In a designated camping area; and

(2)

Under a valid camping permit.

(b)

All camping permits will be issued at designated county park or recreation areas by the county parks representatives no earlier than the first day of the desired period of continuous and actual occupancy.

(c)

Reservations for camping permits will only be accepted in accordance with the rules and regulations issued by the county and the county parks and recreation director.

(d)

Payment of the camping permit fee, in full, is required at the time the camping permit is issued or, if a reservation for a camping permit has been made, at the time the reservation is made.

(e)

The maximum period of consecutive overnight camping in any county park or recreation area is 14 days within any 30-day period.

(f)

No camper shall leave a set-up camp unattended for more than 12 hours.

(g)

At least one adult, 18 or older, shall accompany each camper group.

(h)

No campfires should be left unattended.
