For the purposes of this article, schematic plans shall be prepared and furnished in triplicate to the owner for review. The architect or engineer will be notified when the review is complete, and a meeting will be arranged by the owner. The meeting shall include all persons involved in the project. The owner will give written authorization to the architect or engineer to proceed with design development drawings within budgeted funds. The respective boards shall review schematics for all major building projects.

(Ord. No. 2015-03, 3-16-2015)
