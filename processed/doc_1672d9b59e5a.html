At any stage in the proceedings, including before the probable cause hearing, the owner, upon showing proof of ownership, may obtain possession of the removed vehicle by paying the towing fee and any storage charges, or by posting a bond for double the amount of such fees and charges to the tow truck operator or county having custody of the removed vehicle. Upon retaining possession of a vehicle, the owner entitled to the possession of the vehicle shall not allow or engage in further violations of this chapter.
