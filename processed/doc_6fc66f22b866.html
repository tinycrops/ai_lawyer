Under this article, the grantee shall have no recourse whatsoever against the county or its officials, boards, commissions, agents, or employees for any loss, cost, expense or damage arising out of any provision or requirements of the franchise or because of the enforcement of this article or the franchise. However, nothing in this section is intended to deny the grantee the right to contest determinations made by the county.
