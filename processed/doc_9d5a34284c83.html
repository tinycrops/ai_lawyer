(a)

Any person having knowledge of or keeping, feeding, harboring or possessing a stray animal must surrender the animal to the animal services division.

(b)

All stray animals delivered to animal services shall be taken to the county animal shelter. If an owner does not redeem the animal within three business days, the animal shall become the property of the county and may be disposed of in accordance with this chapter.

(Ord. of 8-17-2009; Ord. No. 2014-04, 6-16-2014; Ord. No. 2023-02, 3-6-2023)
