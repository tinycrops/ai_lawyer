(a)

The Lake Norman Marine Commission shall have a governing board of five commissioners. Each commissioner shall serve a five-year term, with commissioners to serve overlapping terms so that one commissioner appointment is made each year. The Catawba County Board of Commissioners shall appoint two commissioners, and the boards of the other three counties shall appoint one each. These appointees shall serve their terms until September 30. Appointments shall be made each October 1 by the respective board of the four counties in rotation by county in the order set out for initial appointments.

(b)

If the death of a commissioner, resignation, incapacity or inability to serve occurs, as determined by the board appointing such commissioner, or removal of the commissioner for cause, as determined by the board appointing such commissioner, the board affected may appoint another commissioner to fill the unexpired term.
