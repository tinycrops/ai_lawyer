Before the board of county commissioners revokes a license issued pursuant to this division, or if the board of county commissioners determines reasonable grounds exist to deny an application for a license pursuant to this division, the board of county commissioners shall cause a written notice to be sent by certified mail to the licensee affected or applicant affected at the address stated in the license or application. This notice shall advise the affected party of a right to appear before the board of county commissioners presenting any evidence relevant to such revocation or denial and for the purpose of hearing all evidence submitted and examining or cross examining any person providing such evidence.
