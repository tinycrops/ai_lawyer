It shall be unlawful for any person to promote, sponsor, instigate, organize or take part in, any parade or procession in the unincorporated areas of the county involving more than three automobiles or 12 pedestrians, unless a permit for the parade or procession shall first have been secured from the county manager. Expressly excepted from the operation of this article shall be bona fide funeral processions, activities of fire departments, police departments, or other valid functions by governmental agencies. A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
