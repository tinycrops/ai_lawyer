It shall be unlawful for any person to massage or to offer to massage the private parts of another for hire. Massage means the manipulation of body muscle or tissue by rubbing, stroking, kneading or tapping, by hand or mechanical device. Private parts means the penis, scrotum, mons veneris, vulva or vaginal area. The provisions of this section shall not apply to licensed medical practitioners, osteopaths or chiropractors, or persons operating at their discretion in connection with the practice of medicine, chiropractic, or osteopathy.
