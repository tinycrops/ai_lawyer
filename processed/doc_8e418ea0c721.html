All records and written documents pertaining to the facilities shall be maintained and made available to the public in accordance with the provisions of G.S. 132-1 et seq. These documents shall include, but not be limited to, monitoring and inspection records, documents pertaining to licensing actions, license applications and enforcement actions and private and public haulers and their respective clientele lists; provided, however, that this provision shall not conflict with the provisions regarding maintenance and retention of records as specified in the federal Resource Conservation and Recovery Act. The provisions regarding confidentiality of records as specified in the federal Resource Conservation and Recovery Act are hereby adopted and incorporated in this section by reference. A copy of the federal Resource Conservation and Recovery Act are available in the office of emergency management.
