(a)

Any person shall be subject to all penalties allowed by law if the person:

(1)

Violates or fails to comply with the provisions of the Fire Code;

(2)

Violates or fails to comply with any order made under the Fire Code;

(3)

Builds in violation of any detailed statement of specifications or plans submitted and approved under the Fire Code or any certificate or permit issued thereunder; or

(4)

Fails to comply with such an order as affirmed or modified by the fire marshal or by a court of competent jurisdiction, within the time affixed.

(b)

In addition to any civil penalties or remedies that may apply, any person who commits any violation listed in subsection (a) of this section may be subject to criminal penalties for a misdemeanor pursuant to G.S.
