(a)

The organization shall consist of the following:

(1)

An emergency management coordinator, and such assistants and other employees as are deemed necessary.

(2)

The employees, and resources of all county departments, boards, institutions, and councils will participate in the emergency management activities. Duties assigned to county departments shall be the same or similar to the normal duties of the department, where possible.

(3)

Volunteer personnel and agencies offering service to and accepted by the county.

(b)

Pursuant to G.S. 166A-19.15(b) the office of the county manager shall be the emergency management agency, and the county manager shall be the emergency management coordinator, responsible for the organization, administration and operation of the emergency management agency, subject to the direction and guidance of the county board of commissioners.

(c)

The coordinator shall designate individuals to whom emergency management duties may be delegated, subject to budget appropriations approved by the board of commissioners.
