Any person desiring to engage in the business, trade or profession of masseur or masseuse or the operation or carrying on of any of the businesses, trades, professions, occupations or callings mentioned in section 14-76 shall, before engaging in such business, trade, profession, occupation or calling, file an application for a license addressed to the board of commissioners. Such application shall be in writing and shall set forth the following:

(1)

Name and address of applicant. The name and address of the applicant must be given, and if such applicant is a corporation, the names and the addresses of such corporation and its officers must be given.

(2)

Qualifications. The qualification of the applicant must be plainly stated and must be submitted, together with required exhibits annexed to the application proving such qualifications.
