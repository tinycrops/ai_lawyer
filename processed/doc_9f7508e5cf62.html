The county manager shall appoint the director of the library and such person must have a professional librarian certificate issued by the secretary of cultural resources pursuant to G.S. 125-9, under regulations for certification commission pursuant to the provisions of G.S. 143B-67. The director of the library and other employees of the library shall be employees of the county and shall be subject to its personnel code or policy and shall be subject to all of the benefits accruing to and responsibilities of county employees.
