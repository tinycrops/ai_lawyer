All generators and all facilities that treat, store or dispose of hazardous waste shall notify the county within 90 days of commencing business operations of their hazardous waste operations, in order to obtain registered status. Notification will be deemed made upon receipt by the office of the director of emergency management of written details of the hazardous operations. This provision shall be inapplicable to new generators and facilities that commence business operations after August 5, 1987.
