(a)

Fees for inspections required by this article shall be determined by resolution of the board of commissioners. An inspection fee schedule shall be filed with the clerk to the board of commissioners and in the fire marshal's office for public inspection.

(b)

Inspection fees shall be paid within three days, as specified in the billing or notice of the amount of the fee.
