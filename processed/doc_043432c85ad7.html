(a)

A violation of this chapter shall be a misdemeanor punishable by a fine of not more than $50.00 or by imprisonment for not more than 30 days or by both such fines and imprisonment.

(1)

The imposition of a penalty for any violation shall not excuse the violation nor shall the violation be permitted to continue.

(2)

Such persons shall be required to correct or remedy such violations or defects within a reasonable time, and when not otherwise specified, the application of the above penalty shall not be held to prevent the enforced removal of prohibited conditions.

(3)

Proof of such unlawful act or failure shall be deemed prima facie evidence that such act is that of the owner or other person in control of the premises.

(b)
