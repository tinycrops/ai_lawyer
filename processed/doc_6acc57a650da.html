It shall be unlawful for any person to interfere with, hinder or molest the animal control department or its agents or animal control officers or veterinarians in the performance of any duty authorized by this chapter, or otherwise specifically provided. When requested, an officer of the county sheriff's department or the municipal law enforcement agency shall accompany an animal control officer investigating a suspected dangerous or vicious dog. A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
