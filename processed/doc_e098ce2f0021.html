(a)

No person licensed as a massagist under section 18-91 shall massage or treat any person or engage in the business or profession of massage before 8:00 a.m. or after 12:00 p.m., prevailing time.

(b)

No person licensed under section 18-71 shall admit customers or prospective customers, remain open for business, or allow or permit or condone any massage or treatment of any person upon the premises before 8:00 a.m. or after 12:00 p.m., prevailing time.

(c)

No person in charge of managing a massage business upon the premises shall allow, permit or condone any massage or treatment of any person before 8:00 a.m. or after 12:00 p.m., prevailing time.

(d)

A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
