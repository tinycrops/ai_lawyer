(a)

Each member of the commission shall be appointed by the board of commissioners, which may request recommendations from the commission.

(b)

Each youth athletic playing district which participates in three or more county youth sporting leagues (presently baseball, softball, football, basketball and indoor soccer, but which may change from time to time) is entitled to have a member representative appointed to the commission. If a youth athletic playing district meets those criteria, it may petition to have a representative appointed by the board of commissioners. If the board of commissioners determines that the youth athletic playing district meets the criteria, the board of commissioners will appoint a representative as provided in subsection (a) of this section. If a youth athletic playing district should cease to participate in the minimum number of county youth sports leagues, then that youth athletic playing district shall lose its representation on the commission and its representative shall be removed by action of the board of commissioners.

(c)

Each youth athletic playing district shall be represented by a community organization responsible for sponsoring and organizing youth participation in its district. Should the community organization change, whether by removal of an organization by the board of commissioners and its replacement by another organization, or by any other circumstances, then the board of commissioners may remove the representative of the youth athletic playing district so affected, and appoint a new representative as provided in subsection (a) of this section for the remainder of the unexpired term.
