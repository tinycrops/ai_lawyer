Appeals from final decisions of the EO that there is reasonable cause to believe that a violation of the article has occurred may be appealed to a panel consisting of an assistant county manager, a staff attorney, and the strategic partnerships director. Appeals must be made in writing and delivered to the clerk to the board of the Buncombe County commission within ten days of receipt of the decision.
