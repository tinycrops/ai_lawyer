The emergency management director shall be authorized to permit entrance to areas that are the subject of a ban for the removal or securing of property, provided such permission is provided in writing and is limited to the purposes specified in this section. The emergency management director shall require evidence or verification of ownership or possessory right of the property to be secured. Permissible entry into an area that is the subject of a ban or for which entry is denied shall be solely at the risk of the person so entering. Permits issued shall be displayed upon request of any county officer or employee responsible for the enforcement of ordinances.
