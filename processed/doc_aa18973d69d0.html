(a)

During the installation of new water lines, fire hydrants shall be installed every 500 feet.

(b)

Private roadways, driveways, and areas adjacent thereto shall be properly maintained and kept free of all obstructions that would reduce or limit access of fire apparatus and other emergency vehicles.

(c)

Repeated false alarms due to alarm malfunctions, improper care of fire protection equipment or testing of systems may result in a fine not to exceed $100.00 per alarm.

(d)

Street numbers that are visible from the road shall be posted at each residence. Structures off of main roads will have the number posted at the road and at the structure.
