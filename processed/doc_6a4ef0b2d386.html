Unless the development agreement specifically provides for the application of laws subsequently enacted by the county or municipality, the laws applicable to development of the property subject to a development agreement are those in force at the time of execution of the agreement, except as provided for in G.S. 153A-344.1(e). In the event state or federal law is changed, the development agreement is subject to the provisions of G.S. 153A-359.7(c).
