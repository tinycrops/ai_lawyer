(a)

It shall be unlawful for any person to pull flowers or fruits, whether wild or cultivated, vegetable or plant life, cut down trees or saplings, break limbs or branches of trees or remove bark from trees, or injure shrubbery or plants or to girdle any tree within any county park or recreational facility.

(b)

It shall be unlawful for anyone to hunt, destroy, trap or otherwise capture or take any animal, wildlife, migratory bird or other fowl within a county park except when specifically authorized by the director of parks and recreation.
