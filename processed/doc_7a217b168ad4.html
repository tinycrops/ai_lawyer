(a)

Damage, removal or disposal without authority. All pictures, plaques, and memorabilia located in the hallways and offices of the county courthouse are not to be damaged in any way, placed in trash receptacles, or removed from the courthouse without the express written consent of the county board of commissioners.

(b)

Inapplicability of section. This section shall not apply in cases where the person engaged in such acts is the owner of the item that is damaged, discarded, or removed.

(c)

Penalty for violation of section. A violation of this section shall constitute a Class 3 misdemeanor, punishable by a fine of up to $500.00 or a maximum 30 day's imprisonment as provided in G.S. 14-4.
