All fires, excluding personal smoking materials, are prohibited except in designated areas. Park and other authorized county employees, their agents, and contractors may prohibit fires in designated areas and smoking when a fire hazard makes such action necessary. A competent person 16 years old or older must continuously tend any fire from the time it is kindled until it is extinguished.
