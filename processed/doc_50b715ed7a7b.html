Unless otherwise provided by this chapter or state law, any animal impounded at the animal shelter, or other appropriate facility, must be redeemed within three business days of impoundment. Any person attempting to redeem an animal must appear in person at the animal shelter and make payment of all applicable costs, fees and penalties.
