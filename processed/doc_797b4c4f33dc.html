(a)

The county has established a solid waste collection system consisting of centrally located bulk-storage container units. Such container units are alternately known as green boxes or by the trade name, dumpster. This system is intended for the collection of customary items of household solid waste and is serviced periodically by front-loading solid waste collection vehicles.

(b)

The purpose of this division is to define and abate nuisances within the county and to define, regulate, prohibit or abate acts, omissions or conditions detrimental to the health, safety or welfare of the citizens of the county and the peace and dignity of the county.

(c)

It is the intent of this division to accomplish this purpose through the regulation of the county's solid waste containers and container sites, with such regulation including, but not limited to:

(1)

Persons allowed to use such facilities;

(2)

Solid waste materials allowed to be placed into solid waste containers; and

(3)

Standards of conduct and procedure to be followed when using solid waste containers and container sites.
