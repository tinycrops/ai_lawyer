As chief administrative officer, the county manager shall be responsible for all liaison between the board of commissioners and all department heads and employees of the county. Except where the board, by majority vote, shall choose to deal directly with an administrative service, the appointment, removal and supervision of officers and employees in the administrative service of the county shall be the express duty of the county manager.
