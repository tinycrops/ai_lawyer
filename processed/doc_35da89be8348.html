(a)

The catchlines of the sections of this Code are intended as mere catchwords to indicate the contents of the section and shall not be deemed or taken to be a part of the section.

(b)

The history or source notes appearing in parentheses after sections in this Code are not intended to have any legal effect but are merely intended to indicate the source of the matter in the section.

(c)

Cross references, state law references and editor's notes which appear in footnote form are provided for the convenience of the user and have no legal effect.
