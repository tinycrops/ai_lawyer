The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Advisory board means the county agricultural advisory board created pursuant to G.S. 106-739 to administer the farmland preservation program.

Agricultural conservation easement (sometimes herein "ACE") shall have the meaning defined in G.S. 106-744(b).

Chairperson means chairperson of the advisory board.

Board of commissioners means the Buncombe County Board of Commissioners.

Conservation agreement means conservation agreement as same is defined in G.S. 121-35(1).
