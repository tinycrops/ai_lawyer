(a)

All roads in the county's jurisdiction shall be named in accordance with G.S. 153A-240.

(b)

The county's ordinance for naming and renaming streets, adopted May 1, 1984, shall apply and is incorporated in this article by reference.

(c)

The county's road name sign installation policy, adopted August 2, 1988, shall apply and is incorporated in this article by reference.

(d)

Developers of new roads within the county are hereby encouraged to contact county officials prior to naming such roads to ensure compliance with state and local laws.

(e)

Developers are also encouraged to contact county officials and make arrangements to install road name signs according to county policy.
