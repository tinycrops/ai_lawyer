The land records manager will be responsible for the interpretation and administration of this article, including:

(1)

Assigning all numbers for properties and buildings.

(2)

Maintaining address records for each property and building.

(3)

Recommending change of existing addresses when necessary to facilitate sequential house numbers along a road.

(4)

Designating individual unit addresses within multiple housing units in conformity with this article.

(5)

Assisting the public in complying with the requirements of this article.
