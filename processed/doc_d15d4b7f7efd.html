Specific requests for recommendations may be made from time to time by the board of commissioners to the planning board. Such requests may require that the recommendations be submitted within a stated time period. However, if after expiration of the time period, no recommendation shall have been submitted, the board of commissioners may take such final action with respect to the matter submitted as it deems wise.
