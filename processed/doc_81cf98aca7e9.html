The purpose of this chapter is to regulate the storage, collection, and disposal of solid waste in the county. This ordinance is adopted pursuant to the authority contained in G.S. 153A-121, 153A-132.1, 153A-136, 153A-274 through 153A-278, and 153A-291 through 153A-293, and 130A-309.09, 130A-309.09A, 130A-309.09B, and 130A-309.09D. Unless otherwise indicated, the chapter applies to both publicly-owned and privately-owned municipal solid waste management facilities located in the county.
