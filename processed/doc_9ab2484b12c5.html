(a)

The board will meet quarterly on the second Thursday of March, June, September and December. A 72-hour written notice will be given with time and location noted.

(b)

The board will address grievances as described in section 26-60.

(c)

The board will evaluate all requests for changes, deletions or additions to first responder program or this article and make recommendations to the county board of commissioners

(d)

The board will provide audit and review for the first responder program.
