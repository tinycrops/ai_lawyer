(a)

The materials deposited into solid waste containers shall be limited to customary items of household solid waste which otherwise comply with the provisions of this division and any other applicable regulations. Such containers shall not be utilized by persons engaged in the collection of solid waste for compensation. Further, such containers shall not be utilized by persons for the deposit of commercial, industrial, contractual or institutional solid waste.

(b)

In no event, however, shall this restriction be construed to deny the use of such containers to temporary residents of the county, provided that such persons are otherwise in compliance with the provisions of this division and any other applicable regulations.
