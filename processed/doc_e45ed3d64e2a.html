(a)

Through individual contracts with municipalities, the county provides wastewater treatment facilities to both residential and business property owners in unincorporated areas of the county. Each wastewater treatment facility is operated pursuant to a municipal ordinance created in conformance with applicable laws and approved by the appropriate controlling state agency.

(b)

Every property owner whose property is connected to a municipality's wastewater treatment system, pursuant to the county's contract with the municipality, is subject to all applicable terms and conditions of the municipality's sewer use ordinance, which is adopted by the county and incorporated in this section by reference.

(Ord. No. 2006-07, 6-15-2006; Ord. No. 2017-11, 9-18-2017; Ord. No. 2020-17, 10-19-2020)
