Application for the license required by this division shall be addressed in writing to the sheriff and shall set forth the following:

(1)

The name and address of the applicant. If the applicant is a corporation, the addresses of the corporation and its officers.

(2)

A plain statement of compliance with qualifications for application as set forth in section 14-49, together with required exhibits attached.
