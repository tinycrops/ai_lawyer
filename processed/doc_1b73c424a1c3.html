Smoking, the use of e-cigarettes, and the use of any tobacco products is prohibited:

(1)

In any building owned, leased, occupied, or operated by the county;

(2)

In any vehicle owned, leased, or operated by the county; and

(3)

On any grounds that are owned, leased, occupied, or operated by the county.
