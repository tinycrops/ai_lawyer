It shall be unlawful for any person to loiter in or upon the school grounds or public buildings belonging to the Hickory City Schools Board of Education between the hours of 6:00 p.m. and 7:00 a.m., except as follows:

(1)

It shall not be unlawful for any person to be on such grounds during such hours while attending any school function, including athletic events, parent-teacher meetings, club meetings or any other event authorized by the Hickory City Schools Board of Education or by a principal employed by the Hickory City Schools Board of Education.

(2)

It shall not be unlawful for individuals having business with the Hickory City Schools Board of Education to be on the grounds during such hours when upon the grounds for specific business or school purposes.

(3)

It shall be permissible for individuals to be on or about such premises between such hours when specific permission is given by the superintendent of schools for the Hickory City Schools Board of Education or by a principal employed by the Hickory City Schools Board of Education.

(4)

It shall be permissible for any person to be on or about the premises during such hours for the use of playgrounds, athletic fields, and tennis courts, provided that the person does not loiter in or around the school buildings.
