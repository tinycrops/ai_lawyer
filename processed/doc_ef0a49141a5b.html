(a)

Where feasible, residential connections to county-owned sewer utilities are required to connect to municipal water infrastructure except as set out in section 42-59. Residential connections to county-owned sewer utilities that are not connected to a municipal water system will be billed for sewer services at a rate of 6,000 gallons per month.

(b)

All nonresidential connections to county-owned sewer utilities are required to connect to municipal water infrastructure except as set out in section 42-59.

(Ord. No. 2020-17, 10-19-2020)
