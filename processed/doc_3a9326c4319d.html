Parking facilities at a mass gathering shall be provided off public roadways sufficient to serve the reasonably expected requirements at a rate of up to 100 passenger cars per acre or 30 buses per acre. One parking space for cars shall be provided for each four people to attend. Camper class vehicles shall park in the camping area provided in section 42-67. Parking on shoulders of public highways shall not be permitted and temporary signs shall be erected by the applicant to so indicate.
