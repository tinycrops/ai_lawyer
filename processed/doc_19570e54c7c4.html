The annual solid waste user fee as time to time assessed by the board of county commissioners shall be billed with county property taxes, shall be payable in the same manner as property taxes, and, in the case of nonpayment, may be collected in any manner by which delinquent personal or real property taxes can be collected. The fees shall be a lien on the real property described on the bill that includes the fee.
