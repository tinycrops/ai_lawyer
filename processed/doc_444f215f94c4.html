(a)

The solid waste collector agreement shall include at least the following terms:

(1)

Name and address of the licensee.

(2)

A statement that the licensee shall only deliver solid waste produced in Cleveland County for disposal at the Cleveland County Landfill.

(3)

A statement that the licensee shall have all necessary tarping, covers, and/or diapers to prevent roadside litter.

(4)

A statement that the licensee shall carry all necessary insurance, including, but not limited to, commercial automobile, general liability, and workers compensation insurance.

(5)

A statement that the licensee shall have drivers licensed under North Carolina law operating all vehicles being drive by the licensee of its staff.

(6)

A statement that the licensee shall obey the speed limit inside of the Cleveland County landfill.

(7)

A statement that failure to comply with the terms of the solid waste collector agreement may result in revocation of dumping privileges at the Cleveland County Landfill.

(b)

Additional licensing requirements.

(1)

The solid waste director or his designee may inspect equipment used by a licensee for the permitted activities.

(2)

Vehicles and containers used for the collection and transportation of solid waste shall be covered, leak proof, durable, and easily cleaned.

(3)

Vehicles shall display plainly the company/owner's name and address or phone number.

(4)

Vehicles and containers used for the collection and transportation of solid waste shall be loaded and moved in such a manner that the contents will not fall, leak, spill, or scatter. Such vehicles and containers shall be covered to prevent the blowing of material. If spillage or scattering of materials occurs, the materials shall be recovered by the licensee and returned to the vehicle or container and the area properly cleaned.

(5)

Licensees may not dispose of waste from outside of Cleveland County in the Cleveland County Landfill.

(6)

Licensees are subject to all applicable terms of this chapter.

(7)

The licensee shall serve every person who contracts with it for solid waste collection in such a manner than the licensee does not cause the person to be in violation of this article. The licensee shall not discriminate against any person for contract purposes in violation of the Civil Rights Act, as amended, on the basis of race, color, creed, religion, national origin, physical impairment, sex, or any other protected class.

(8)

Failure to comply with any of the terms in this section, or any of the terms in the solid waste collector agreement, or any applicable law or regulation, may result in revocation or suspension of a license.
