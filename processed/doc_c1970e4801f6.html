(a)

The periodic review to assess compliance of the developer at least every 12 months by the zoning administrator or appropriate representative of the county or municipality is required.

(b)

All procedures and process shall be set forth in the development agreement as required by G.S. 153A-349.8 pertaining to compliance assessment; material breach by the developer; notice of breach; and cure of breach, or modification, or termination of the development agreement.

(c)

In the event that the county enters a development agreement outside of the land use jurisdiction of the county, a notification of the compliance assessment and findings of the review shall be provided to the county and said requirement be provided for in the development agreement.
