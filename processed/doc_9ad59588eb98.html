(a)

The terms of office of the members of the county industrial facilities and pollution control financing authority are on file and available for public inspection in the office of the county clerk.

(b)

Each of the commissioners of the authority shall be a qualified elector and resident of the county, and each shall continue in office for the assigned term or until his successor shall be duly appointed and qualified. Each of the commissioners and their successors will serve at the pleasure of the board of commissioners and may be removed, with or without cause, by the board of commissioners.

(c)

None of the commissioners shall be an elected official of the county.
