No masseur or masseuse or any person or party engaging in any of the businesses licensed by this article shall engage in such business, trade, profession, occupation or calling except within and between the hours of 8:00 a.m. and 10:00 p.m.; nor shall any operator of a massage parlor or establishment or business enumerated and not specifically excepted under this article, operate the same except within and between such hours. Violations of this or any other section of this article shall be considered under this article as an offense. A violation of this provision shall constitute a Class 3 misdemeanor as set forth in G.S. 14-4. The maximum fine for a violation under this chapter is $500.00 or a maximum 30 day's imprisonment.
