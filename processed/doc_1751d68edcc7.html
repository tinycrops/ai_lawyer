(a)

Hearings. All quasi-judicial hearings shall be conducted in accordance with G.S. 160D-406 or as amended.

(b)

Decisions. All quasi-judicial decisions shall be made in accordance with G.S. 160D-406 or as amended.

(c)

Filing of decisions. Quasi-judicial decisions are effective upon filing the written decision with the clerk assigned to the decision-making board. The decision of the board shall be delivered within a reasonable time by personal delivery, electronic mail, or first-class mail to the applicant, landowner, and any person who has submitted a written request for a copy prior to the date the decision becomes effective. The person required to provide notice shall certify to the local government that proper notice has been made, and the certificate shall be deemed conclusive in the absence of fraud.
