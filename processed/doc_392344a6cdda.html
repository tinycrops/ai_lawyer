In addition to all other fees and charges which may be imposed under this division, an acreage assessment shall be payable in an amount established by the board of commissioners from time to time for each acre of property with respect to which sewer service is to be provided, and shall be payable from time to time as the acreage, with respect to such services to be provided, shall increase. Property, with respect to which such service is to be provided, shall refer to improved property and all property appurtenant thereto. A single-family dwelling shall be charged an assessment fee for one-fourth acre regardless of the actual amount of property owned by the resident.
