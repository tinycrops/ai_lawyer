This article applies to all scrap metal companies, or individuals doing business as a recycling center, in the jurisdiction of the county that deal in all metals, to include copper, aluminum, lead, titanium, steel, iron, gold, silver, or all other recyclable metals for the purpose of business as a secondhand dealer, or recycling center, where materials are exchanged for monies or other compensation.
