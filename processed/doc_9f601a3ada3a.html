Prior to construction or extension of a water and/or sewer system under this division, application shall be made, upon such form as may be prescribed therefor, to the county for a review of plans and written approval allowing such construction or extension. The county may require, as a part of such application, submission of plans and specifications setting forth the details of the proposed construction or extension. It shall be unlawful to make such construction or extension without written approval.
