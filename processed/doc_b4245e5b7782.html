(a)

It shall be unlawful for any unauthorized person to enter upon or trespass upon, after regular business hours, any county-owned property that is properly posted in a manner reasonably likely to come to the attention of unauthorized users or intruders, with notice not to enter upon the premises as provided in G.S. 14-159.13.

(b)

The county manager by and she is hereby authorized to cause to be erected and posted appropriate and necessary signage upon request of the director of physical facilities or the departmental director whose premises are being improperly used during or after normal business hours.

(c)

Penalty for violation of section. A violation of this section shall constitute a Class 3 misdemeanor, punishable by a fine of up to $500.00 or a maximum 30 day's imprisonment as provided in G.S. 14-4.
