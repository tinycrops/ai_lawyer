(b)

In all areas and locations where water pressure exceeds 100 pounds per square inch, the property owner shall provide such pressure-regulating valves as may be necessary to reduce the pressure in the piping system on his property to a safe level. It is recommended, but not required, that such pressure-regulating valves be installed where water pressures exceed 75 pounds per square inch.
