(a)

All or any portion of a franchise granted under this article may be terminated or suspended for any of the following reasons:

(1)

Loss of state solid waste management permits by the franchise holder;

(2)

Noncompliance with the franchise, collection, transportation, and disposal fee schedules authorized by the board of county commissioners;

(3)

The attempted transfer of the franchise without prior approval of the board of commissioners;

(4)

Failure to render prompt and effective service to persons within the area in which the franchise holder has a franchise to operate; or

(5)

Failure to comply with any provisions of this chapter or applicable state and local regulations.

(b)

Complaints or grievances may be brought to the attention of the county by submission in writing to the solid waste director.
