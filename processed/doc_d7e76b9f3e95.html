Upon the determination that any provision of articles I through VI of this chapter is being violated, the code enforcement supervisor shall send, within five working days, a written notice by registered mail or personal delivery to the person responsible for such violation, indicating the nature of the violation, civil penalty, as applicable, and ordering the action necessary to correct it. Additional written notices may be sent at the code enforcement supervisor's discretion.
