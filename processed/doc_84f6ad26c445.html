There is hereby imposed and levied within the county a six percent room occupancy and tourism development tax authorized by ratified Chapter 658 of the 1989 Session Laws (Regular Session, 1989), effective July 19, 1989. The tax hereby imposed and levied shall apply to the same extent and be subject to the same limitations as are set forth in said ratified chapter and section 70-31 of the Code of Ordinances of the county be and the same is hereby amended consistent herewith.
