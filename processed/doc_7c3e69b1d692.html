It shall be unlawful for any person to violate any of the provisions of this article or of the regulations or plans issued pursuant to the authority contained in this article, or to willfully obstruct, hinder or delay any member of the emergency services organization in the enforcement of the provisions of this article or any regulations or plan issued pursuant to this article. Any person who violates any provision any ordinance or declaration enacted or declared pursuant to this article shall be guilty of a Class 2 misdemeanor in accordance with G.S. 14-288.20A.
