(a)

No building permit shall be issued until an official house number has been assigned for a lot.

(b)

No certificate of occupancy shall be issued by the county zoning administrator until such road address numbers are properly displayed.

(c)

Owners or occupants of buildings already constructed which do not comply with this article will be notified and requested to meet these requirements within 60 days from the date of the notification. A warning notice will be issued after 60 days if the requirements have not been met. If the owner or occupant does not comply voluntarily with this article within 30 days of delivery of a warning notice by registered or certified mail or by hand delivery to the building in violation, enforcement action pursuant to G.S. 153A-123 may be initiated.
