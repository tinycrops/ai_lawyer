(a)

It shall be unlawful for individuals to act in a disorderly manner and/or create a noise which would result in an unreasonable disturbance to others. Acting in a disorderly manner includes, but is not limited to, a boisterous, abusive, threatening, and indecent or disorderly conduct.

(b)

It is unlawful to enter or remain within the limits of county property while under the influence of alcohol or controlled substances.
