The owner or operator of a manufactured home park shall submit a map of the manufactured home park to the planning department. The map shall indicate the following:

(1)

The location, name and width of each roadway within the manufactured home park; and

(2)

Identification of each lot, by number, within the manufactured home park.

This map shall be made available to the county planning department upon a 30-day written request.
