The commission may recommend to the board of commissioners acceptance of any proper grant, gift, bequest or donation of any personal or other property offered or made for recreation purposes, and with the board's approval may accept any grant, gift, or devise of real estate. Any gift, bequest of money or of other personal or real property, or any grant or devise, shall be held by the department and used and finally disposed of in accordance with the terms or conditions under which such grant, gift or devise is made and accepted.
