It shall be unlawful to park any vehicle in or on any county-owned parking lot or parking facility in violation of or contrary to the rules established by the board of commissioners for the use of the parking lots or facilities, or to use the parking lots or parking facilities in any manner other than that designated by the board of commissioners. A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
