(a)

Unlawful. Except as provided in subsection (b) of this section, it shall be unlawful for any person to possess, consume, display or sell any alcoholic beverages, malt beverages or unfortified wine upon the premises of or within any property owned, leased or operated by Buncombe County.

(b)

Exception; permit required; permit conditions. Any individual, organization or group may submit an application for a permit to the director of parks and recreation on the form supplied by the parks and recreation department. If the director shall conclude that the issuance of a permit for the possession, consumption, display or sale of any alcoholic beverages, malt beverages or unfortified wine can be authorized for a specific time and area, will not hinder or impede any regularly established use of the county property and will not adversely affect the county's ability to care, protection or maintenance or create a nuisance on the property, the director of parks and recreation may grant permission for such use. If the director of parks and recreation shall determine otherwise, the application for a permit shall be denied; provided, however, that any permit granted shall be for use only in a clearly designated area and no alcoholic beverages, malt beverages or unfortified wine may be removed from that area into other areas or facilities. Further, individuals not designated in the permit application shall not be allowed in the designated area during the time set forth in the permit. In addition the applicant is required to get the necessary permits from the State of North Carolina. Alcoholic beverages, malt beverages or unfortified wine shall not be allowed in any boats or other rental or mechanical equipment.
