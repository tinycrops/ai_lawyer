It shall be unlawful for any person to willfully fail or refuse to comply with any final order or direction of the building inspector or board of commissioners. Any person violating this article shall, upon conviction, be punished as provided by state law for the violation of county ordinances. Every day such person shall willfully fail or refuse to comply with any final order or direction of the building inspector or the board of commissioners shall constitute a separate and distinct offense.
