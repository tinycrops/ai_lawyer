(a)

This section is enacted pursuant to authority in G.S. 153A-170.

(b)

Parking at the county justice center and the adjoining government center shall be in accordance with the following:

(1)

Parking shall be limited to properly designated spaces on the paved lot surrounding the justice center and the government center, which are not restricted as provided in subsections (b)(2) and (3) of this section.

(2)

It shall be unlawful to permit any vehicle to stand, stop or park, except when necessary to avoid conflict with other traffic or in compliance with directions of a police officer or traffic control device, in any of the following places:

a.

Any place where the vehicle would block the use of a driveway or access road.

b.

On any sidewalk or parkway.

c.

At any place where official signs prohibit parking.

d.

At parking places designated for persons with mobility disabilities.

e.

At any fire lane as designated by a yellow curb.

(3)

It shall be unlawful for any person without a mobility disability or not using a wheelchair to park in any space reserved for such persons with disabilities; however, a driver of a vehicle used in transporting such persons with disabilities may park in such spaces.

(c)

The chairperson of the board of commissioners shall cause signs to be posted in all areas where parking is limited or prohibited, indicating such limitations or prohibitions.

(d)

The City of Newton, by and through the Newton Police Department, is expressly authorized to enforce this section.
