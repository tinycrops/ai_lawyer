(a)

The county's solid waste containers are financed by revenues generated within and/or allocated expressly to the county. Accordingly, it shall be unlawful for any nonresident of the county to deposit solid waste into the containers.

(b)

Due to the diverse sources and composition of solid waste generated on property located within the boundaries of the county and the specific characteristics of each, the board of commissioners shall possess the authority to restrict the use of solid waste containers to certain persons within the county on the basis of source and/or composition as specified in sections 58-62 and 58-63. In no event, however, shall any person who is otherwise in compliance with the provisions of this division, and any other applicable regulations, be arbitrarily or capriciously denied the right to use the containers.
