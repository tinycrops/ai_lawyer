(a)

No person shall gain admittance or otherwise come on to county park or recreational property for which a charge is made without first paying the designated fee, if any.

(b)

No person shall remain within any park or recreational property if that individual refuses to pay the required fee, if any, to enter and use the facility (park or recreational property).

(c)

No person shall enter into or remain in any park or recreational property when it is closed to the public.
