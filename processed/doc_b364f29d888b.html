(a)

The county board of commissioners or their designee shall post signs that meet all the requirements in section 56-4.

(b)

The county board of commissioners or their designee shall remove all ashtrays and other smoking receptacles.

(c)

The county board of commissioners or their designees who see an individual using a tobacco product who is in violation of this chapter must ask that individual to stop using the tobacco product or e-cigarette.
