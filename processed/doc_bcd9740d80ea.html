(a)

In considering proposed changes in the text of this chapter or in the zoning map, the board of commissioners acts in its legislative capacity and must proceed in accordance with the requirements of section 17 [of this ordinance].

(b)

Unless otherwise specifically provided in this chapter, in acting upon major development permit requests or in considering amendments to this chapter or the zoning map, the board of commissioners shall follow the regular, voting and other requirements as set forth in other provisions.
