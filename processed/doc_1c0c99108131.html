(a)

The provisions of this chapter shall apply to all lands as designated by the county except for the lands within the municipal boundaries of the county. Municipal jurisdiction may be included into the territorial application by the adoption of the ordinance from which this chapter is derived, as amended, by the municipality and by mutual agreement between the county and the municipality.

(b)

All territory which may hereafter be included within the municipal boundary of any municipality shall be continued in the county's existing jurisdiction of this chapter until otherwise classified by the governing board of the affected municipal government.
