The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Massage parlor means any place or business where massagists are employed to apply or offer to apply manual or mechanical massage or other treatment to another for a salary or fee.

Massagist, masseur and masseuse mean one who applies or offers to apply manual or mechanical massage or similar treatment to another for a salary or fee.
