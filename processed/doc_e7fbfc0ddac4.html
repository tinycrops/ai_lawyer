(a)

There shall be a technical review committee consisting of at least one (1) representative from each of the following agencies:

(1)

County environmental health;

(2)

County building inspections;

(3)

County planning department;

(4)

North Carolina Department of Transportation;

(5)

Cleveland County Planning Board, chairman;

(6)

Public utility provider.

(b)

The U.S. postal service, NCDEHNR - Division of Environmental Health and Natural Resources, school superintendent or other agencies as the board of commissioners or planning board may deem necessary, shall have an opportunity to review and make recommendations concerning proposed subdivision plats.

(c)

The committee shall establish a regular meeting schedule and shall meet frequently enough so that it can make recommendations in conformity with section 4.10 [of this ordinance].

(d)

The chairman of the county planning board shall also chair this committee.

(e)

Minutes shall be kept of all committee meetings.
