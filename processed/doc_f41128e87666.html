(a)

The parking of motor vehicles, or otherwise obstructing a required or designated fire lane shall be prohibited at all times as required by the North Carolina State Building Code: Fire Prevention Code. This prohibition does not apply to emergency vehicles while engaged in an emergency response.

(b)

Any person who parks in a fire lane shall be subject to a $50.00 penalty issued by either the Catawba County Fire/Rescue Division or by a law enforcement officer.

(Ord. No. 2010-08, 9-7-2010)
