All applications for projects to be funded by the revenue sharing program during the next fiscal year must be submitted and reviewed in accordance with the utility investment advisory committee (UIAC) procedures. Emergency projects or projects involving economic development are considered outside the UIAC process.

(Ord. No. 2006-07, 6-15-2006; Ord. No. 2017-11, 9-18-2017; Ord. No. 2020-17, 10-19-2020)
