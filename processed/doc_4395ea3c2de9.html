It shall be the responsibility of all persons who are working on approved water line extensions or additions to notify the county of the location of the work, the duration of the interruption of water service, and a description of the facilities rendered temporarily useless. Notice shall be given at least 72 hours in advance of starting to work, and the work shall not begin without such notice. Upon objection to the interruption, the county shall have the final decision regarding the time when the work may be done. Such requirement of notice shall not apply in cases where bona fide emergencies exist, and repairs are being made by or under the supervision of the county.
