It shall be unlawful for any person to authorize, allow, or cause to be emitted from any property or source under his control any noise that is unreasonably loud, raucous or disturbing so as to frighten, pose a danger to the health of, or seriously disturb any person of ordinary sensibilities who:

(1)

If the noise emanates from a source located on private property, is located on other property; or

(2)

If the noise emanates from a source located on a street or other public property, is located on private property or the street or other public property.

(Ord. No. 2005-01, 1-18-2005; Ord. No. 2017-12, 9-18-2017)
