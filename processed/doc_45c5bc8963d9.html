It shall be unlawful for any person to resell water purchased from the county without the express consent of the board of commissioners; provided, however, that this shall not apply to water used by such purchaser in any manufacturing or bottling process, nor shall it apply to water served or sold to customers in containers having a capacity of not more than 50 gallons.
