(a)

Authority to limit number of ambulances. The board of commissioners may, from time to time, limit the number of ambulances that may be operated in the county, and may limit the number of ambulances that may be operated by each franchised operator.

(b)

Authority to determine areas of operation. The board of commissioners may, from time to time, determine the areas of the county that may be served by each franchised operator.

(c)

Authority to establish charges. The board of commissioners may, from time to time, establish a schedule of rates, fees and charges that may be charged by franchised operators.
