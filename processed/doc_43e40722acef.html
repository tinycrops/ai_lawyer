It shall be unlawful for any person to enter or remain in the park/recreation facility except during those hours of operation that it is open to the general public. Park/recreation facility hours of operation will be posted and may be changed from time to time by the board of commissioners. Cabin and tent renters are exempt. A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
