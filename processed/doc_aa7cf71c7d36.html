The board may terminate or suspend all or any portion of a franchise for any of the following reasons:

(1)

Loss of the franchisee's license to operate as a solid waste collector;

(2)

Failure of the franchisee to comply with the authorized fee schedules;

(3)

Failure of the franchisee to render prompt and effective service to persons within his service area;

(4)

Failure of the franchisee to comply with any provision of this division or applicable rules of the department.
