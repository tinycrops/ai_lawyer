The following words, terms, and phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Abandon means to forsake a dog or cat in a street, road, or public or private place without providing for the care, sustenance, protection, and adequate shelter of such animal.

Adequate shelter means an enclosed area accessible by an animal, of sufficient size and nature so as to provide the animal with reasonable protection from adverse weather conditions.

Aggressive dog means a dog that has communicated by its actions an intention, habit, tendency or propensity to do something harmful such as:

(1)

On more than one (1) occasion attempted to bite when unprovoked; or

(2)

Habitually or repeatedly chases, snaps at, or otherwise aggresses pedestrians, joggers, leashed dogs being walked on a public roadway or property, bicycles or vehicles;

(3)

Approached a person of an adjoining property in a vicious or terrorizing manner in an apparent attitude of attack on more than one (1) occasion not less than twenty-four (24) hours apart.

Animal bite means a puncture or laceration, caused by the teeth of an animal.

Animal services director means the person who is appointed by the county manager to oversee all animal services and animal control activities carried out by the county except for those that are otherwise provided for by statute. If at any time the county does not have an animal services director, the rights and duties of the animal services director shall be exercised by the county manager or his designee.

Animal services officer means any employee by delegation of the animal services director, performs duties related to the investigation and enforcement of violations of this chapter.

Animal shelter means any premises designated by the county for the purpose of impounding and caring for all animals found running at large or otherwise subject to impoundment in accordance with the provisions of this chapter.

At large means any animal shall be deemed to be at large when it is off the property of its owner and not under restraint of a competent person.

Attack training facility means any person, group of persons, partnership or corporation engaged in boarding, breeding, selling or training dogs or other animals in mode of attack.

Cat means a domestic feline of either sex.

Commercial dog and/or cat breeder means any person who owns, has custody of, or maintains female dogs or cats over the age of six (6) months that are capable of reproduction and kept primarily for the purpose of breeding and selling the offspring as pets.

Commercial purpose means that the motivation of action is for monetary gain (for-profit).

Community cat shall mean any free-roaming, unowned cat living in an outdoor environment that may or may not be a part of a cat colony. A community cat may or may not be feral.

Dangerous animal means any animal that has demonstrated a fierce or dangerous propensity or tendency to do any act which may endanger persons or property.

Dangerous dog means any dog that has:

(1)

Assaulted, bitten, attacked, or inflicted severe injury on a human being without provocation on public or private property;

(2)

Killed or injured a pet or domestic animal without provocation while off the owner's property;

(3)

Been declared dangerous by the animal services director or animal services advisory board.

(4)

Been owned or harbored primarily or in part for the purpose of dog fighting, or trained for dog fighting.

Exceptions: No dog is dangerous pursuant to this definition if, at the time the threat, injury or damage was sustained, the person attacked was committing a willful criminal trespass upon the premises of the owner or keeper or was teasing, tormenting, abusing or assaulting the dog or has in the past teased, tormented, abused or assaulted the dog or was committing or attempting to commit a crime. Nor shall a dog be considered dangerous pursuant to this definition if it has attacked or injured a pet or domesticated animal in defense of an attack by another animal or if it is protecting or defending its young.

Dog means a means a domestic canine of either sex.

Domestic animal means any of various animals such as dogs, cats, horses, sheep, cattle, goats, hogs, poultry, etc., domesticated by man so as to live and breed in a tame condition.

Exposed to rabies means an animal that has been bitten by, or been exposed to, any animal known or suspected to have been infected with rabies.

Guard and sentry dog means a dog trained for the primary purpose of enhanced aggression.

Health director means the director of the county health department or authorized representative, as described in G.S. Â§ 130A-41.

Hunter means a person who purchases and keeps current a North Carolina or South Carolina hunting license.

Impound or impoundment shall mean to seize and hold in the custody or possession an animal by the animal services department.

Kennel, dealer, breeder, or pet shop means any person, group of persons, partnership or corporation engaged in buying, selling, breeding, or boarding animals.

Livestock shall mean all animals of a domesticated, agricultural nature, including, but not limited to, horses, cattle, sheep, goats, llamas, swine, alpacas, zebra, camels, and ratites.

Neutered male means any male animal which has been castrated.

Owner means any person, group of persons, firm, partnership or corporation owning, keeping, having charge of, sheltering, feeding, harboring or taking care of any animal. The owner is responsible for the care, actions and behavior of his animals. In the event that the owner or keeper of an animal is a minor, the parent or guardian of such minor shall be held liable for noncompliance with the provisions of this chapter.

Person means person as defined by G.S. Â§ 12-3(6).

Pet means a domesticated animal kept for pleasure rather than utility.

Primary enclosure means any structure used to immediately restrict an animal or animals to a limited amount of space.

Provocation means any action by a person which causes an animal to immediately engage in a response that is motivationally different from the response it was engaged in just prior to the action of the person.

Rabies vaccination means the administration of anti-rabic vaccine approved by the United States Bureau of Animal Industry, the state department of agriculture and the state board of health.

Restraint means the act of keeping an animal under the immediate physical control of a person capable of restraining the animal, on or within a vehicle being driven or parked, by means of a collar and leash, or by passive means of control, such as inside a residence or building, fence, kennel, or other confinement device with defined partitioned area.

Secure enclosure means an enclosure, appropriate for the species, from which an animal cannot escape and prevents other animals from entry or access. Neither a motor vehicle nor a chain attached to a fixed object shall constitute a secure enclosure.

Severe injury means any physical injury that results in broken bones or disfiguring lacerations or required cosmetic surgery or hospitalization.

Spayed female means any female animal which has had ova hysterectomy.

Stray means any dog or cat that appears stray, homeless or unwanted, and any dog that is not displaying a valid rabies tag.

Tether means tying out or fastening an animal on a chain, leash or other like device so that it can range only within a set radius.

Trespass means the intentional, unauthorized entrance onto someone's property. The cause of the individual to be on the property and any other relevant circumstances shall be considered to determine whether a trespass has occurred. A child under the age of seven (7) shall not be deemed to be a trespasser.

Veterinary hospital means an establishment maintained and operated by a licensed veterinarian for surgery diagnosis and treatment of diseases and injuries of animals.
