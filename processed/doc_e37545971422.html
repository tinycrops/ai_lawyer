Appropriated monies for the recreation department may be from general fund revenues derived as profit of a local sale or power; from water, sewer or other charges and fees; from all tax sources permitted under the state recreation enabling act; from monies from any local, general fund sources other than taxation; from profits derived through any operation conducted by the county; from all profits of department revenue-producing activities; and from any gift, bequest, grant or devise made to the county, the commission and/or department.
