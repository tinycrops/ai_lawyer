All appeals which may be taken from decisions or orders of the officer pursuant to section 3.5-46 of this article shall be heard and determined by the board of adjustment. As the appeals body, the board shall have the power to fix the times and places of its meetings, to adopt necessary rules of procedures and any other rules and regulations which may be necessary for the proper discharge of its duties. The board shall perform the duties prescribed by section 3.5-46 and shall keep an accurate journal of all its proceedings.
