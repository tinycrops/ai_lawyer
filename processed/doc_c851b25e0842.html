(a)

For the purposes of this section, no-wake speed is idle speed or slow speed creating no appreciable wake.

(b)

It is unlawful to operate a vessel at greater than no-wake speed within 50 yards of a boat launching area, bridge, dock, pier, marina, boat storage structure, or boat service area on the waters of Lake Norman.

(c)

This section is enforceable under G.S. 75A-17 as if it were a provision of G.S. ch. 75A.
