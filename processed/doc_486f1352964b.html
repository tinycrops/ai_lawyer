(a)

Any act constituting a violation of the provisions of this article or a failure to comply with any of its requirements shall subject the offender to a civil penalty of $100.00 per violation. If the offender fails to pay this penalty within ten days after being cited for a violation, the penalty may be recovered by the county in a civil action in the nature of debt.

(b)

Each day that any violation continues after final notification by the administrator that such violation exists may be considered a separate offense for purposes of the penalties and remedies specified in this article.

(c)

This article may also be enforced by any appropriate equitable action. Such remedy may include a court order of abatement as part of a judgment in the case. The abatement order may include removal of violation and other actions required to make the property comply with the provisions of this article at the owner's expense.
