The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Advisory board means the Burke County Agricultural Advisory Board.

Board of commissioners means the Burke County Board of Commissioners.

Chairperson means the chairperson of the Burke County Agricultural Advisory Board.

District means voluntary agricultural district as established by this chapter.
