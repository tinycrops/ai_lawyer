All notices from grantee to the county pursuant to this chapter and the franchise shall be to the county manager or his/her designee. Grantee shall maintain with the county, throughout the term of the franchise, an address for service of notices by mail. The grantee shall be required to advise the county of such address(es) and telephone numbers and any changes thereof.
