(a)

The preliminary assessment resolution may be adopted without notice by the board of commissioners after receiving the petition filed pursuant to this article. The preliminary assessment resolution shall set the time for the public hearing upon the resolution which shall be held not less than three weeks and not more than ten weeks from the date of enactment of the resolution.

(b)

The determination of the total costs by the board of commissioners is conclusive.

(c)

There shall be a 90-day period, commencing upon the date of the enactment of the preliminary assessment resolution, during which the validity of the petition may be contested.
