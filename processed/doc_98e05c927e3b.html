(a)

That the carrying of a concealed handgun is hereby prohibited in all county owned or maintained buildings, offices and property.

(b)

That the exceptions to this policy shall be the same as those enumerated for courthouses in G.S. 14-269.4.

(c)

The following parks shall be exempt from this section:

(1)

Walnut Island River Park;

(2)

Ledges River Park;

(3)

Sandy Bottoms River Park;

(4)

Hominy Creek River Park;

(5)

Bent Creek River Park;

(6)

Jean Webb River Park;

(7)

Glen Bridge River Park;

(8)

Corcoran-Paige River Park;

(d)

That notice of this policy shall be conspicuously posted in all county owned or maintained buildings, offices and property in order to comply with the provisions of G.S. 14-415.11(c).

(e)

Penalty for violation of section. A violation of this section shall constitute a Class 3 misdemeanor, punishable by a fine of up to $500.00 or a maximum 30 day's imprisonment as provided in G.S. 14-4.
