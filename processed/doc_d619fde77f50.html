The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Business or profession of massage means and includes the massage or treatment of any person for a fee or in expectation of a gratuity from the person massaged.

Massage means the manipulation of body muscle or tissue by rubbing, stroking, kneading or tapping by hand or mechanical device.

Massage business means any establishment or business wherein massage is practiced, including establishments commonly known as health clubs, physical culture studios, and massage studios or parlors.

Massagist means any person engaged in the business or profession of massage.
