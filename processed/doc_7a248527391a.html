The grantee may, with the agreement of the county, use county-owned conduit or other facilities for any portion of its cable television system. The consideration for the use of county conduit or other facilities shall be as stated in a contract for such usage. A fair and reasonable fee for the use of county facilities shall be established in a contract for such usage, and may be adjusted at the periodic performance evaluations. The county shall indemnify and hold grantee harmless from any claim or action found to be caused by the intentional or negligent act or omission of county, its employees, agents, or subcontractors.
