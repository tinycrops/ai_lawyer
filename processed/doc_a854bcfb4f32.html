The provisions of this article with respect to the original issuance of a license shall not apply to those engaged in the businesses, trades, professions, occupations, or callings enumerated in section 14-76 who have been regularly licensed by the county or who have been so engaged in the county for a period of three years or more prior to the adoption of this article; provided, however, that the provisions of this article shall apply to all such businesses, trades, professions, occupations, or callings from and after the date of the adoption of this article, and all the businesses, trades, professions, occupations or callings shall be properly licensed as provided in this article.
