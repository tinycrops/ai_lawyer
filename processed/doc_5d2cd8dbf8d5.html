All emergency response vehicles within the county shall carry the U.S. Department of Transportation Emergency Response Guidebook for Hazardous Materials (DOT-P 5800.2). Operators of emergency response vehicles shall be trained in the use of this manual. A copy of this manual shall be made available for public inspection at the office of the director of emergency management.

(Ord. No. 18853, art. V, 9-15-87)
