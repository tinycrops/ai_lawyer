(a)

If an animal dies while under observation for rabies, the head of such animal shall be submitted to the county health department for shipment to the North Carolina State Laboratory of Public Health for rabies diagnosis.

(b)

The carcass of any animal suspected of dying of rabies shall be surrendered to the county animal services director. The head of such animal shall be submitted to the county health department for shipment to the state laboratory of public health for rabies diagnosis only if the animal was involved in a bite. The state lab will not test specimens if a bite has not occurred.

(Ord. No. 2020-04 , 8-18-2020)
