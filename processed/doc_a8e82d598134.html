There shall be no open burning of garbage by any person. Garbage, as defined in section 11-28 of this Code, means all putrescible waste, including animal offal and carcasses and recognizable industrial byproducts, but excluding sewage and human waste.

(1)

Fine for violation. Any person violating the terms of this section shall pay a fine in the amount of one hundred dollars ($100.00).

(2)

A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
