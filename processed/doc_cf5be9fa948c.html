The developers of new subdivisions, mobile home parks, industrial parks and other similar developments shall erect the appropriate street signage in accordance with guidelines set by the county. Upon written request by the developer or owner, the county will authorize the developer to erect the approved signage, provided that the placement and signs meet all current county sign specifications. A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
