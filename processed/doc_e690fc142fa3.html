(a)

It shall be unlawful for any person to occupy a canoe, kayak or any other boat in any portion of the Broad River within the Greenway without complying with all state and federal regulations regarding the wearing and usage of life vests and preservers.

(b)

Boating upon any portion of the Broad River within the Greenway shall be subject to such regulation as may reasonably be imposed by officials of the Greenway for the safety of boaters and others.

(c)

A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.

(Ord. of 5-6-97; Ord. of 1-20-22(1))
