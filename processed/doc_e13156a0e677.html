A solid waste collector franchised under this division shall present to each prospective customer, in advance of any agreement with that customer, a schedule of his fees, as authorized by this division, to be charged. All solid waste shall be removed from the customer's premises at least once a week, provided the customer is no more than 30 days in arrears in payment of the required collection fees.
