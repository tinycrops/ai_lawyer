The availability of recreational opportunities for citizens of all ages is a subject of general interest and concern. The board of commissioners therefore declares that the public good and general welfare of the citizens of this county require adequate and safe parks and recreational areas. This article shall apply to parks and recreational areas owned or leased by the county or owned by other persons and available for use by the general public, if the private owners have requested and the board of commissioners has approved, by resolution, the applicability of this article.
