Where land to be subdivided is found by the subdivision administrator to be subject to the conditions of flooding, improper drainage, severe erosion, slides, or to have other characteristics which pose an ascertainable danger to health, safety or property, the subdivider shall take measures necessary to correct said conditions and eliminate said dangers.
