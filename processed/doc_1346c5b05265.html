The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Commission means the advisory recreation body to the department, manager and board of commissioners.

Department means the recreation department of the county.

Manager means the administrator, whether he is the county manager or other person, to whom is delegated the administrative duties and responsibilities of the county.

Recreation means those activities which fall within the general classification areas of athletics: sports, games and parks.

Structures and areas means the outdoor and indoor areas and structures on or in which people derive opportunity for the pursuit of happiness through recreation, whether or not these areas and structures are owned, leased, borrowed, controlled or operated within the county.
