Unless otherwise specified in this chapter, all notices or civil penalties required by this chapter to be sent or delivered shall be made by personal service, certified mail, first class mail, or affixing the notice to the last known residence of the person who violated this chapter. Failure to comply with any valid notice shall be punishable as a class III misdemeanor.
