The county fire marshal shall maintain a record of all fires and facts concerning the same, including injuries, deaths, rescue of persons and statistics as to the extent of such fires and the damage caused thereby, and whether such losses were covered by insurance, and if so, in what amount. Such records shall be made daily from the reports made by the technical inspectors under the provisions of this ordinance. All such records shall be public.
