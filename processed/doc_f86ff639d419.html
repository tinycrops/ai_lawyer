Nothing in this article shall:

(1)

Preclude, prohibit or nullify any existing or future arrangements by and between the county and the Burke County and Eastern Burke Rescue Squads or any other person for the provision of emergency back-up service under the terms and provisions of such arrangements.

(2)

Prohibit or make unlawful the transporting of anyone by an unfranchised ambulance operator:

a.

From a location outside the county to a medical facility within the county; or

b.

From a medical facility within the county to a location outside the county.

(3)

Impose any requirements or require any practice that is inconsistent with the provisions of G.S. 131E-155.
