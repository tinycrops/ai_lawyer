All notices from the grantee to the county pursuant to this article and the franchise shall be to the county manager. The grantee shall maintain with the county, throughout the term of the franchise, an address for service of notices by mail. The grantee shall also maintain with the county a local office and telephone number for the conduct of matters related to the franchise during normal business hours. The grantee shall be required to advise the county of such addresses and telephone numbers and any changes thereof.
