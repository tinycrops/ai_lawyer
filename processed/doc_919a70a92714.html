(a)

Recyclable corrugated cardboard, that is corrugated cardboard as that term is commonly understood, which businesses and dealers in this area are willing to purchase when it is delivered to them, is a solid waste for which a surcharge will be imposed for disposition at the county waste management facility effective January 1, 1992.

(b)

The county waste management facility will provide a place and method for citizens and businesses to deposit cardboard for the purpose of recycling.

(c)

Any haulers disposing of waste at the county facility which contains five percent or more of cardboard, by weight or by volume, shall pay a surcharge in addition to the regular tipping fee based on the following:

(1)

For the first violation, a written warning.

(2)

For the second violation, a surcharge equal to four times the then current tipping fee.

(3)

For the third and any subsequent violation, a surcharge equal to nine times the then current tipping fee.
