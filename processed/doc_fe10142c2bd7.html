The planning board shall consist of nine members appointed at large by the board of commissioners to serve three-year terms. Each member of the board shall be a legal resident of the county at the time of his appointment and shall maintain his residency during his term of appointment. The initial members shall serve staggered terms. Their successors shall be appointed to full three-year terms. Vacancies occurring for reasons other than expiration of terms shall be filled by the board of commissioners as they occur for the period of the unexpired term. Faithful attendance at the meetings of the planning board is considered a prerequisite for the maintenance of membership on the planning board.
