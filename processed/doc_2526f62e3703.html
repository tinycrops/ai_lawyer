(a)

The position of director of recreation is hereby created. The executive for the department shall be the director of recreation for the county who shall also work with the manager as one of his department heads. The director of recreation may be either a full-time or part-time employee, in the discretion of the board of commissioners.

(b)

Candidates for the position of director of recreation shall, upon interviews with the recreation commission, be nominated to the manager for his selection and employment. Such person shall be, in the opinion of the commission and manager, fully trained and qualified to lead, organize, direct, administer and maintain a satisfactory recreation program for the county.

(c)

An assistant director of the department of recreation and/or such other persons as may be deemed necessary for the supervision, operation, and maintenance of the recreation program and the recreation system, including parks, centers, play areas, and other recreation facilities and areas, may be employed and discharged by the director of recreation in accordance with personnel policies of the county and with approval of the manager.
