The chairperson may direct and compel the evacuation of all or part of the population of the county, to prescribe routes, modes of transportation, and destination in connection with evacuation; and to control ingress and egress of a disaster area, the movement of persons within the area, and the occupancy of premises therein. Details of the evacuation may be set forth or amended in a subsequent declaration which shall be well publicized.
