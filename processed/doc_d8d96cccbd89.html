(a)

Overview. Effective in July 1993, the county industrial development commission (IDC) and office of business development merged. The name of the corporation was changed to the Catawba County Economic Development Corporation to more accurately reflect its status as a nonprofit corporation and to distinguish it from industrial development commissions and economic development commissions under state law.

(b)

Articles of incorporation. At its meeting on December 20, 1993, the board of commissioners adopted the amended economic development corporation articles of incorporation, 190.02, and bylaws, 191.01. The county and the cities of Hickory, Conover, Claremont, Newton, Maiden, Catawba, Brookford and Long View fund and appoint members to the economic development corporation board.
