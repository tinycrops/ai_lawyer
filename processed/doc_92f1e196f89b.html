There is hereby created an Animal Services General Trust Fund (trust fund) for the purpose of accepting and disbursing gifts, grants, and awards of money paid to Cleveland County for the use and benefit of dogs and cats in Cleveland County.

(1)

The trust fund account is to be self-perpetuating from year to year unless abolished by the county board of commissioners.

(2)

The animal services director shall have authority to expend trust account funds for the betterment of the Cleveland County Animal Shelter and Adoption Center and the animals of Cleveland County.

(3)

Gifts, grants, and awards received from public and private donors shall be deposited in the control trust fund account created in this section and shall at all times be kept separate and apart from the general funds, such that they will more to the use and benefit of animal control.

(4)

Gifts, grants, and awards not immediately used may be invested in accordance with the county's investment policies or practices.

(5)

Funds not expended at the close of the fiscal year shall be carried forward into the next year.

(6)

Gifts, grants, and awards to the trust fund account shall be received by animal services and delivered to the finance officer, who shall deposit them as determined by subsection (3).

(7)

Any gifts, grants or awards received subject to a condition shall be expended strictly in accordance with such condition.

(Ord. Of 9-21-21(2))
