It is the basic function of the commission to promote recreation for county citizens and, in so doing, the department of recreation and its commission are authorized to aid and assist agencies (in line with reasonable and legally correct policies recommended by the commission and accepted by the county) which include public; private; commercial; those which are quasi-public in character, and which, although public in nature, are not under the board of commissioners, such as schools, churches, hospitals, military installations, orphanages, commercial recreation, business and industrial agencies; as well as civic, neighborhood and service groups in their recreation interest and needs.
