The following agencies are hereby authorized to give notice of violation of the provisions of this article:

(1)

The county sheriff's department;

(2)

The county emergency management department;

(3)

The county fire marshal's office;

(4)

The county inspections department;

(5)

The county attorney's office; or

(6)

Other agencies as designated by the board of commissioners.

The notice shall be in writing, directed by name to the property owner, and shall set forth the action necessary for the offender to be in compliance.
