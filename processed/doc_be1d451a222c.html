In the event there is any confusion concerning the interpretation of this chapter, the following definitions and/or rules of construction shall apply:

(1)

The term "county board" shall mean the Burke County Board of Commissioners.

(2)

The term "trustees" or "board of trustees," unless a different meaning is clearly indicated, shall mean the board of trustees appointed by the county board. The board of trustees shall act as advisors to the library director.
