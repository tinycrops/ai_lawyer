(a)

All lots in any subdivision around Lake Hickory or Lake Rhodhiss shall be served by a publicly operated central water and sewer system to help protect these watercourses if any portion of the subdivision is located within 250 feet of the reference line. The reference line for each of the listed lakes is: Lake Hickory, 935 feet above mean sea level full pond; and Lake Rhodhiss 1,000, feet above mean sea level full pond. All applicable floodplain regulations shall still apply to these areas as necessary.

(b)

In addition, the lots covered under this section will be required to meet the requirements of article XII of the county's zoning ordinance.

(c)

This requirement shall not apply to lots subdivided as sites for single-family dwellings which are two acres or more in size not to lots subdivided as sites for single-family dwellings which are 1.5 acres or more in size and are to be served with either a publicly operated central water or central sewer system.
