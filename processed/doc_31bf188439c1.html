(a)

The board of commissioners of the county has adopted a fee schedule for this chapter which is incorporated herein by reference, is certified by the clerk to the board (appendix I of an ordinance adopted April 6, 1999) and is kept for public inspection in the office of the clerk to the board and the county animal shelter. The board shall have the authority to change the fee schedule from time to time by resolution.

(b)

Animal owners desiring to keep their animal in an unaltered state must pay differential fees pursuant to the fee schedule adopted as specified in subsection (a) above.

(Ord. Of 9-21-21(2))
