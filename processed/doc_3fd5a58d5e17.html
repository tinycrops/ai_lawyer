Any person appointed to a board or commission by the board of commissioners, which board or commission was not created by the county, yet which has a designated membership for a county representative, shall submit a report of each meeting to the clerk to the board of commissioners. Such appointee has a responsibility to the board making such appointment and must notify the clerk immediately if the board or commission takes any action that may affect the county. Such report shall be due within one week after each regular or special meeting. If the appointee is absent, the report must include a review of the proposed agenda and state the reason for such absence. If more than one county appointee is on the board or commission, the senior member shall be responsible for the report, except, in his absence, the report shall be filed by another county-appointee present at the meeting.
