The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

County government building means a building owned, leased as lessor, or the area leased as lessee and occupied by the county government.

County vehicle means a passenger-carrying vehicle owned, leased, or otherwise controlled by the county government and assigned permanently or temporarily by county government to county government employees, agencies, institutions, or facilities for official county government business.

E-cigarette means any electronic oral device that provides a vapor of nicotine or other substance and which simulates smoking through its use or through inhalation of the vapor through the device, and includes an oral device that is composed of a heating element, battery, or electronic circuit and marketed, manufactured, distributed, or sold as an e-cigarette, e-cigar, e-pipe, or any other product name or descriptor, if the function of the product meets the definition of an electronic oral device.

Employee means a person who is employed by an employer or who contracts with an employer or third person to perform services for an employer, or who otherwise performs services for an employer with or without compensation.

Grounds means an unenclosed area owned, leased, or occupied by county government.

Smoking means the use or possession of a lighted cigarette, lighted cigar, lighted pipe, or any other lighted tobacco product.

Tobacco products means any product that contains tobacco and is intended for human consumption.
