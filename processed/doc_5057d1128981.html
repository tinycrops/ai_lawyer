This article does not apply to the following:

(1)

Veterinary clinics in possession of such animals or reptiles for treatment or rehabilitation purposes.

(2)

Nonresident circuses for no longer than one seven-day period per each separate location where such circus is held within the county per calendar year.

(3)

Persons temporarily transporting such mammals or reptiles through the county, provided that such transit time shall not be more than 24 hours.

(Ord. of 8-17-2009; Ord. No. 2014-04, 6-16-2014; Ord. No. 2023-02, 3-6-2023)
