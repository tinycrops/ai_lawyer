If during any inspection the fire marshal determines that the fire department is not meeting the minimum standards as set forth in this article, a notice of deficiency must be given in writing by the fire marshal to the fire chief and at least two officers of the corporation of the fire department. This notice shall specify the period of time within which the deficiencies must be corrected, provided that the length of the period of time shall be reasonable and in any event shall not be less than 15 days. At the end of the specified period, an inspection shall be made by the fire marshal to determine if the deficiencies have been corrected. If the deficiencies have not been corrected, the fire marshal shall notify the Cabarrus County Board of Commissioners with a recommendation to contract with another department to provide service until deficiencies have been corrected.

( Ord. No. 2020-50 , 12-21-20)
