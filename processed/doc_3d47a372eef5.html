The declaration of state of emergency shall include a definition of the area constituting the emergency area. The emergency area of a state of emergency declared shall not include any area within the corporate limits of any municipality, or within any area of the county over which a municipality has jurisdiction to enact general police-power ordinances, unless the municipality's governing body or mayor consents to or requests the state of emergency's application.
