The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this chapter, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Firing range means an unenclosed, uncovered area designed, set up or intended for the discharge of firearms at designated targets, consisting of more than two firing stations or points and more than two targets. Targets shall include fixed targets and moving targets, including skeets, traps and clay pigeons.
