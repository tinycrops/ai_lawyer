Public hearings, as set forth in G.S. 153A-323, are required prior to the county entering into a development agreement. The notice for the public hearing shall include the following:

(1)

Specify the location of the property subject to the development agreement.

(2)

The development uses proposed on the property.

(3)

Specify the location a copy of the development agreement can be obtained.

(4)

When applicable, the notice shall provide the delivery date of public services as tied to the successful performance by the developer in implementing the proposed development.
