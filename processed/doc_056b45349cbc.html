An inspection department, known officially and designated as the department of permits and inspections, is hereby established pursuant to G.S. 160D-402(b), 160D-404(c), and 160D-1102, and shall consist of a building inspector, a plumbing inspector, a heat-air conditioning inspector, an electrical inspector, and such other inspectors or deputy or assistant inspectors as may be authorized by the board of commissioners. The board of commissioners or the county manager may designate a department head.
