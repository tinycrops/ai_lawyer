It shall be unlawful for any person to:

(1)

Break, damage, destroy, uncover, deface or tamper with any equipment or materials belonging to the county and used or intended to be used for the purpose of making measurements, tests or examination of sewage or waste.

(2)

Remove, damage or interfere with the functioning of any pipe, main, cover, building, apparatus or equipment used for the collection, conveyance or treatment of sewage or industrial wastes.

(3)

Place or cause to be placed in any manhole or sewer pipe or main any material which results, or is likely to result, in the stoppage or obstruction of the normal flow of sewage or wastes or otherwise cause damage to the system.
