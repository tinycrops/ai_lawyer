(a)

If a septage management firm is found dumping in violation of this ordinance or local public health rules, the following penalties shall apply:

(1)

First offense: warning and a fine of $100.00.

(2)

Second offense: a fine of $500.00.

(b)

Violations of this article will be reported to all agencies governing septage management.

(c)

The public health director may bring a civil proceeding to enforce this article or abate a public health nuisance, and the superior court may, upon hearing and for good cause, enjoin the continuance of the condition violating this article, irrespective of all other remedies at law as provided by G.S. 130A-18 and 130A-19.

(d)

Remedies provided in this article are cumulative and nonexclusive.
