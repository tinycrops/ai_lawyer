It shall be the duty of the planning board, in general, to:

(1)

Make studies of the county.

(2)

Prepare objectives to be sought in the physical development of the county.

(3)

Prepare and from time to time review and amend a comprehensive, continuous, and coordinated plan for the physical development of the county.

(4)

Develop and recommend policies, ordinances, administrative procedures and other means for carrying out plans in a coordinated and efficient manner.

(5)

Advise the board of commissioners concerning the use and amendment of means for carrying out plans.

(6)

Exercise any function in the administration and enforcement of various means for carrying out plans the board of commissioners may direct.

(7)

Perform other related duties that the board of commissioners may direct.
