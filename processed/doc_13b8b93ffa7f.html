(a)

Upon receipt of written approval to proceed under this article, the architect, engineer or surveyor shall prepare working drawings which must include the following:

(1)

A firm time schedule within which work must be completed; and

(2)

A projected bid date.

(b)

Upon completion of all plans and specifications, the architect or engineer will submit complete sets to the owner, the department of insurance, and other applicable agencies for final review.

(Ord. No. 2015-03, 3-16-2015)
