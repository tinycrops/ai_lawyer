The purpose of the chapter is to promote agricultural values and the general welfare of the county; increase identity and pride in the agricultural community and its way of life; support the economic and financial health of agriculture; increase protection from nonfarm development and other negative impacts on properly managed farms; and decrease the likelihood of legal disputes, such as nuisance actions between farm owners and their neighbors.

(Ord. No. 2008-16, 10-20-2008; Ord. No. 2022-26, 12-19-2022)
