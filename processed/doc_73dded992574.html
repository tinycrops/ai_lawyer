No yard waste, white goods, used oils, lead acid batteries, antifreeze or aluminum cans shall be disposed of at the county landfill. Those items, and any other items banned from North Carolina landfills in the future, shall be separated from the solid waste and disposed of in a manner consistent with all federal, state, and local regulations.
