(a)

Any person operating under a franchise granted under this article shall present to each customer, in advance of any agreement with that customer, a schedule of his fees, as authorized under this article, to be charged.

(b)

Solid waste shall be removed by the franchise holder from customers' residences at least once per week, provided the customer is no more than thirty (30) days in arrears in payment of such fees.
