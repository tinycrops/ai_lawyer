No person licensed under section 18-71 shall allow or permit any person to massage or treat any person upon the premises operated by the licensee unless the person giving such massage or treatment has complied with all requirements of licensing under section 18-91, including periodic medical examinations by a licensed physician. Violation of this section shall be grounds for revocation of the license to such violator pursuant to this division.
