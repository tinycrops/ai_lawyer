The revenue sharing program is a program to assist local municipalities in extending water and sewer service outside of any incorporated region. The program establishes a partnership between the municipalities and the county, providing affordable service to citizens in need.

(Ord. No. 2006-07, 6-15-2006; Ord. No. 2017-11, 9-18-2017; Ord. No. 2020-17, 10-19-2020)
