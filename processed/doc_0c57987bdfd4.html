Under this division the municipality shall:

(1)

Furnish potable water to customers.

(2)

Perform all operation and maintenance items associated with water and/or sewer lines.

(3)

Perform all testing and reporting mandated by federal, state, and local regulations.

(4)

Service customers by responding to complaints, reading meters, and billing.

(5)

Receive and process all new requests for water and/or sewer service.

(6)

Develop a consistent rate structure for inside/outside customers.

(7)

Implement percentage rate increases for outside customers equal to rate increases for inside customers.

(8)

Apply for federal and state grants, if available.

(9)

Assist the county in the growth plan which mandates controlled and planned growth for the utility system.

(10)

Develop construction specifications compatible with other municipalities to avoid incapability of firefighting equipment during mutual aid situations.

(11)

Repay the loan according to the executed contract specific to the project.

(12)

Assist the county in its effort to expand utility service to areas in need, provided it proves feasible.

(13)

Perform annexation obligations as provided in this article.

(14)

Obtain all easements, including costs, inside and outside of incorporated area.

(Ord. No. 2006-07, 6-15-2006; Ord. No. 2017-11, 9-18-2017; Ord. No. 2020-17, 10-19-2020)
