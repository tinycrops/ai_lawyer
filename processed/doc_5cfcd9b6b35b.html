A person whose alarm permit has been revoked may be issued a new permit if the person:

(1)

Submits an updated application and pays a $100.00 permit fee, and;

(2)

Pays, or otherwise resolves, all citations and fees, and;

(3)

Submits a certification from an alarm business, that complies with the requirements of this article, stating that the alarm system has been inspected and repaired (if necessary) by the alarm business.
