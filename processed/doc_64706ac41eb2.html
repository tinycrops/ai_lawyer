Every adult establishment shall prominently display on the outside of the building or in close proximity thereto a sign identifying the premises as an adult establishment and containing the license number under which it is operated. The sign may also indicate the name by which the establishment does business as, so long as the name does not contain vulgar or offensive language or make references, direct or implied, to specified sexual activities or specified anatomical areas. No other outside signage shall be permitted.
