An applicant under this article, prior to making application for a license, must have the following qualifications:

(1)

Good moral character. The applicant may be male or female, and shall be required to provide written recommendations showing proof of good moral character. In case the applicant is a corporation, such corporation must be created in or domesticated by the laws of this state, and the officers of such corporation shall provide written recommendations showing proof of good moral character.

(2)

Health certificate. Each applicant must furnish a health certificate from a medical doctor which shall accompany such application as an exhibit. If the applicant is a corporation, it shall furnish such certificate to cover its officers, agents or employees who will be actually engaged in and working under the license. Each employee who begins work following the original issuance of a license under this article shall likewise obtain a health certificate. A copy of the application and all certificates shall be furnished to the county sheriff's department.
