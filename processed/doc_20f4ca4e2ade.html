(a)

The director of recreation shall be responsible for the following:

(1)

Administration and supervision of department personnel;

(2)

Administration and supervision of the department recreation program;

(3)

Organization and conduction of recreation activities;

(4)

Maintenance and upkeep of all areas, facilities and equipment;

(5)

Public information and public relations;

(6)

Representation of the department at local, state and national functions and professional meetings;

(7)

Assistance in the securing of financial support for the department;

(8)

Adherence to department and county policies and regulations;

(9)

Arranging for the preparation of and for keeping current a long range plan of recreation program, of area and structure acquisition and development in the following of such plan; and

(10)

Such other functions of public regulation and control as may be within the policy of the county, and approved by ordinance.

(b)

The commission will act in an advisory capacity to the director.
