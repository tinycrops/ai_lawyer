Upon exhaustion of administrative remedies set forth in article IV of this chapter, any abandoned, nuisance, or junked vehicle which is not claimed by the owner or other party entitled to possession will be disposed of by the county or by the tow truck company having custody of the vehicle. Disposition of such a vehicle shall be carried out in coordination with the county and in accordance with G.S. 44A-1.
