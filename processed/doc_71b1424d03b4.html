The funds appropriated by the board of commissioners and budgeted to the recreation department shall be disbursed by the fiscal disbursing officer of the county in the same manner as are funds for other departments of the unit, within the budgeted funds of the department. Funds received from revenue-producing activities of the department shall be deposited with the county fiscal officer to the credit of the department and shall be disbursed by the county disbursing officer, in the same manner as those monies appropriated to the recreation department by the county. All funds from other than county-appropriated sources shall be deposited by the county to the credit of and for the use of the department and shall be disbursed as are other county-appropriated monies, except that funds received from gifts, bequests or from other than the county's general funds, such as revenue produced by the department, or from recreation tax sources of the county, shall be disbursed in accordance with the terms of the voted tax and/or of such gift or bequest.
