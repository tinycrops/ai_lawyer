The fair housing commission shall have jurisdiction to enforce the State Fair Housing Act, G.S. ch. 41A of the North Carolina General Statutes, within the county and shall have jurisdiction over the subject matter of any complaint filed under G.S. 41A-7 relating to property located in the county or to an unlawful discriminatory housing practice occurring within the county.
