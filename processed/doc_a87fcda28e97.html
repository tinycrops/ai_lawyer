The county manager is authorized to appoint one (1) animal services director, who in turn is authorized to appoint one (1) or more animal services officers. The county manager is authorized, with the approval of the board of commissioners from time to time, to determine the amount of salaries, allowances, and expenses within the guidelines of the county personnel ordinance for such animal services director and such animal services officer, such salaries, allowances and expenses to be paid by and through sums appropriated by the board of commissioners.

(Ord. Of 9-21-21(2))
