No deadly weapons may be carried by anyone marching or riding in vehicles or otherwise participating in parades, other than law enforcement officers and members of the armed forces or national guards or armed forces reserves while on duty. Persons in attendance as spectators shall not carry dangerous weapons, except when permitted to carry them in the parade. A violation of this section is punishable as a misdemeanor.
