Pursuant to the provisions of G.S. 143, article 9, entitled "Building Code Council and Building Code," the North Carolina State Building Code is hereby adopted as the law governing and applying to building, general remodeling, and construction of all kinds in the county. Appendix B (Fire Flow Requirements for Buildings) and appendix C (Fire Hydrant Locations and Distribution) of the North Carolina State Building Code: Fire Prevention Code are also hereby adopted.

(Ord. No. 2015-03, 3-16-2015; Ord. No. 2022-24, 11-7-2022)
