In the event any provision, standard or requirement of this article is found to be in conflict with any provision of any other ordinance or code of the county, the provision which establishes the higher standard or more stringent requirement for the promotion and protection of the health and safety of the residents of the county's jurisdiction shall prevail. The state building code, current edition, shall serve as the standard for all alterations, repairs, additions, removals, demolition's and other acts of building made or required pursuant to this article.
