At least ten days before the date set for public hearing under this article, the board of commissioners shall cause to be published a notice that a preliminary assessment resolution has been prepared, and a public hearing will be held at a specified time and place. The notice shall describe generally the nature and location of the improvements. At least ten days prior to the date set for the hearing, the county zoning administrator shall send, by certified mail, to each property owner, as determined from property tax records, a copy of the preliminary assessment resolution indicating the property subject to assessment if the project is undertaken. The county zoning administrator shall file with the clerk to the board a certificate of mailing, certifying that the mailing of notices has been accomplished.
