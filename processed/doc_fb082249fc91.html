(a)

Vehicles; where prohibited. A person shall not drive a vehicle in any park within or upon a safety zone, walk, bridle trial, fire trail, service road or any part of the park area not designated or customarily used for such purpose. Unlicensed motor vehicles, motorbikes or mini-bikes are prohibited.

(b)

Parking.

(1)

An owner or driver shall not cause or permit a vehicle to stand anywhere in any park outside of designated parking spaces, except for a reasonable time to receive or discharge passengers.

(2)

An owner or driver shall not cause or permit a vehicle to stand anywhere in any park unattended or abandoned for longer than 12 hours except by permit.

(3)

An owner or driver shall not leave a vehicle parked unattended or abandoned overnight except by permit.

(c)

Obstructing traffic. A person shall not cause or permit a vehicle to obstruct traffic.

(d)

Speed limit. A person shall not drive a vehicle at a speed greater than the posted speed limit.
