In order to maintain adequate solid waste disposal it is desirable to require review of solid waste disposal facilities proposed in the county. The review will require adherence to the latest edition of the rules and regulations providing standards for solid waste disposal as prepared by the state board of health sanitary engineering division or other regulations adopted by the division. Proposed plans or proposals will be reviewed by the director of community development or his representative. The board of commissioners will then consider approval on submitted proposals at the meeting following the completion of review by the director of community development.
