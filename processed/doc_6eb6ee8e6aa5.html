(a)

The board of trustees shall consist of 12 members, each of whom shall be appointed in the manner prescribed herein or otherwise hold office as set out in subsection (b)(2) of this section. Each appointed member of the board of trustees shall serve a term of three years or until a successor shall have been appointed and duly qualified. A trustee may be reappointed but no appointed member shall serve more than two successive terms (a total of six years).

(b)

In order to create staggered terms, the county board may designate terms of one, two, and three years for the members it appoints so that the terms of three appointed members shall expire each year. The procedure for determining the membership of the board of trustees shall be as follows:

(1)

Qualifications. In making appointments to the library board of trustees, the county board shall take into consideration diversity of opinion and the broadest possible representation. To that end, there should be representation from those governing bodies that provide financial support and there should be at large members from the various areas of the county, including the Morganton area, the Valdese area, and the rural areas of the county.

(2)

Standing membership. The duly elected chairperson of the county board of commissioners, mayor of the City of Morganton, and mayor of the Town of Valdese, by title, shall be a member of the board of trustees by virtue of their elected position, but only during their term of elective office, and it shall not be necessary for the county board to appoint those three members. In sole discretion of each such elected official, a representative may be appointed by such official to serve in their place and when designated in writing by such official shall have all of the authority as if the elected official were personally serving.

(3)

Appointment. The remaining nine members of the board of trustees shall be appointed by the county board; however, in making such appointments, three of the members shall be residents of the City of Morganton, two of the members shall be residents of the Town of Valdese, one member shall be a resident of the Town of Hildebran, two of the members shall be residents of areas other than Morganton, Valdese, and Hildebran and one member shall be an at large member appointed in the sole discretion of the county board. In making such appointments, the sitting trustees of the Burke County Public Library may propose recommendations for consideration by the county board.
