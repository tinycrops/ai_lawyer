Any vehicle parked on any county-owned parking lot or parking facility in violation of this division shall be subject to removal from the parking lot or parking facility by the sheriff of the county or his designated agent, to any storage area or garage. If any vehicle is so removed, the owner of the vehicle, as a condition of regaining possession of the vehicle, shall be required to pay to the county all reasonable costs incidental to the removal and storage of the vehicle and any fine or penalty due for the violation.
