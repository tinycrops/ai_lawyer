In case of the absence or disability of the chairperson, such other person as may be designated by the board of commissioners shall have an exercise of all the powers herein given the chairperson. The line of succession shall be the vice-chairperson, the county manager, the assistant county manager, the emergency management director, and the assistant emergency management director. The emergency management director or his assistants shall have the authority to invoke section 20-23 when immediacy of necessary action precludes contact with the chairperson.
