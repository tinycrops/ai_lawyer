(a)

It shall be unlawful for any person, group of persons, partnership, or corporation to conduct attack training at any location unless such training is conducted within a secure enclosure in the presence of the owner or trainer at all times.

(b)

Any dog or animal trained or being trained in a mode of attack is deemed to be dangerous and is subject to all the provisions of this chapter related to dangerous dogs.

(Ord. No. 2020-04 , 8-18-2020)
