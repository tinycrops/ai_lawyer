(a)

Required. All persons 16 years of age or older entering upon the waters of Lake Julian or contiguous lands for the purpose of fishing shall have in their possession a valid county Lake Julian fishing permit.

(b)

Possession and display; failure punishable as trespassing. The failure of any person 16 years of age or older to possess the permit and display it upon being requested to do so by a county ranger, while such person is upon or in the waters of Lake Julian or contiguous lands leased to the county, for the purpose of fishing, shall be considered trespassing without permission upon the waters and lands, and shall be punishable as provided for by law for the violation of county ordinances and/or trespassing.

(c)

Issuance; terms; fees. The permit shall be issued at Lake Julian by county rangers and shall be granted upon such terms and for such prices as set from time to time by the board of commissioners.
