The following are excepted from the application of sections 16-68 and 16-69.

(1)

Noise or sound emanating from scheduled outdoor, school-sanctioned athletic events.

(2)

Noise or sound of safety signals, warning devices, emergency pressure relief valves or church bells, provided that the church bells are not sounded for more than five minutes in any hour.

(3)

Noise or sound emanating from properly equipped aircraft operated in accordance with applicable federal rules and regulations.

(4)

Noise or sound emitted from any authorized emergency or public safety vehicle.

(5)

Noise or sound emanating from parades, street fairs, festivals or similar events which are conducted, sponsored or sanctioned by the county or a municipality.

(6)

Noise or sound from railroad operations.

(7)

Noise or sound emanating from farm equipment.

(Ord. No. 2005-01, 1-18-2005; Ord. No. 2017-12, 9-18-2017)
