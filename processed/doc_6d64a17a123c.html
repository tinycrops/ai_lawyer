(a)

All connections made to county water mains shall be made in accordance with directions issued by the county.

(b)

Connections between the water main and the meter shall be made by the county or under its direction.

(c)

It shall be unlawful to have plumbing cross-sectioned or so installed that water from the county water system and any other water system may become intermingled.

(d)

The piping and connections beyond the meter shall be kept in good repair by the owner of the premises.
