(a)

It shall be unlawful for any person to:

(1)

Enter or be on a river which is the subject of a ban; or

(2)

Cross or attempt to cross a public street or highway which has been barricaded to prevent entry.

(b)

This section shall not apply to city, state or federal officials acting in the discharge of their official duties or enforcing the provisions of this article.
