The director of recreation shall make the following reports:

(1)

Monthly summary of the county's recreation activities to the commission and to the manager.

(2)

Brief monthly and comprehensive annual community recreation activities report including all phases of department activity, all department-associated and other private and commercial community recreation activities to be made to the commission and, through the manager, to the board of commissioners.

(3)

Brief, monthly and comprehensive annual financial reports to the commission and, in line with policies of the county, to the manager and the board of commissioners.

(4)

Other reports to be submitted to the commission and to the manager as requested or deemed necessary on safety hazards, defective equipment and dangerous conditions, damaged and destroyed property, and accidents occurring on or within recreation areas and facilities.

(5)

His analysis of the commission's consideration and assistance as the department's annual budgetary request is made to the manager and to the board of commissioners.
