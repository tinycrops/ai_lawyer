This article is adopted pursuant to the authority granted to the board of commissioners in G.S. 153A-133 and for the purpose of regulating the production or emission of noises or amplified speech, music, or other sounds that tend to annoy, disturb, frighten or affect the health and well-being of the county's citizens.

(Ord. No. 2005-01, 1-18-2005; Ord. No. 2017-12, 9-18-2017)
