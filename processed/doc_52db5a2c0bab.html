(a)

Application for permits shall be made to the public safety services department and shall be accompanied by plans and specifications showing the proposed work. Sealed architect or engineer drawings of appropriate footer and foundation design shall be submitted when existing structures (which are designed for human occupation) are to be relocated to a new property.

(b)

No permit shall be issued unless the plans and specifications are identified by the name and author thereof.
