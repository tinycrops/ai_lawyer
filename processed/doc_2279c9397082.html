(a)

It shall be unlawful for any person to operate an all-terrain vehicle within the boundaries of the Greenway.

(b)

The term "all-terrain vehicles" shall include, but not be limited to, any two-, three- or four-wheeled vehicle such as motorcycles, motorbikes or other vehicles which are designed for off-road operation. This provision shall not extend to such vehicles which are licensed for highway use and which are used only in designated areas of the Greenway.

(c)

Any violation of this section shall be punishable as a Class 3 misdemeanor.

(Ord. of 5-6-97)
