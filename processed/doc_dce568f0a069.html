The license issued under this division shall be and constitute a personal privilege to conduct the profession or business named in the license, shall not be transferable to any other person or corporation, and shall be construed to limit the person or corporation named in the license to conducting the professional business and exercising the privilege named in the license to the location specified in the license; provided, however, if the holder of a license under this division moves the business for which a license has been obtained to another location, a new license may be issued to the licensee at the new location, for the balance of the license year, upon surrender of the original license for cancellation.
