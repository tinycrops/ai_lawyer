The purpose of the regulations expressed in this article is to provide and enforce a uniform house numbering system along the roadways in the county in order to preserve and promote public health, safety and welfare. Specifically, these regulations are designed to:

(1)

Eliminate duplicate or phonetically similar street names;

(2)

Provide for the uniform marking of streets and roadways, both public roads and private roads open to public use;

(3)

Provide for and assign house numbers for all residents and businesses;

(4)

Establish an official map and listing of all streets and roadways in the county; and

(5)

Establish the procedures by which a street or roadway may be named or have the existing name changed.
