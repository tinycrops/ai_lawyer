Dispatch of first responders and necessary communications will be as follows:

(1)

Only the dispatch will be on the county fire frequency.

(2)

All communications by first responder will be on the ambulance frequency so that the responding ambulance crew will receive all information without the need for an information relay and all non-relevant communications will be kept off the fire frequency. These communications should be limited to the first responder checking en route (10-17) and arrival (10-23), plus any critical medical information.

(3)

Priority dispatch will be utilized.
