The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

Bridle trail means any trail maintained for persons riding on horseback.

Hiking trail means any trail maintained for pedestrians.

Public nudity means a person's intentional failure to cover with a fully opaque covering the person's genitals, pubic area, anal area, or female breasts below a point from the top of the areola while in a public place.

Swimming area means any beach or water area designated by the county as a swimming and bathing area.

Unnecessary stopping means bringing a vehicle to a complete stop at a point other than in a parking place, or other than in conformity with traffic regulations and other than because of a defect in said vehicle.

Vehicle means motor vehicle, mini-bike, motorcycle, motor bikes and the like.
