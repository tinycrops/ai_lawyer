(a)

Definitions. The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this section, shall have the meanings ascribed to them in this subsection, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:

911 means the countywide "911" emergency number system.

Emergency means any situation in which a human life or property is in jeopardy and the prompt summoning of aid is essential.

Willfully and knowingly mean deliberately and intentionally.

(b)

Prohibited acts. It shall be unlawful for any person to intentionally and willfully dial the "911" phone system for reasons other than its intended use or to falsely report a nonexisting emergency, or for any person to knowingly permit any telephone under his control to be used for any purpose prohibited by this section.

(c)

Penalty. Any person who violates the provisions of this section shall, upon conviction, be punished in accordance with section 1-6.
