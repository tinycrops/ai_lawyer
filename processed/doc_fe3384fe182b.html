(a)

An individual applicant under this division shall be of good moral character and shall attach to his application no less than three written recommendations setting forth proof of such good moral character. A corporate applicant shall be created in or domesticated by the laws of this state, and the officers thereof shall be of good moral character. There shall be attached to the application of each corporate applicant not less than three written recommendations setting forth proof of such good moral character of each officer of the corporation.

(b)

Each applicant shall attach to his application a health certificate from a medical doctor. There shall be attached to each corporate application certificates pertaining to its officers, agents or employees who will be actually engaged in and working under such license. Each employee who begins work following the original issuance of a license shall, prior to beginning work, obtain a health certificate, which shall be furnished to the sheriff.
