(a)

There shall be a fee on all checks which are returned for insufficient funds, pursuant to section 3-82.

(b)

Citizens may request copies of any public records of the animal control division for which there shall be a fee, pursuant to section 3-82.

(c)

There shall be a charge for any transportation expenses for livestock/farm animals incurred by the county, which shall be payable by the owner of the animal pursuant to section 3-82.
