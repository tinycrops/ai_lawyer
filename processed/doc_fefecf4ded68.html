The public library shall have such powers and perform such duties as are necessary to provide a library for the citizens of the county. By way of illustration, but not in limitation thereof, the public library shall have all the powers and duties prescribed by state law, specifically including G.S. 153A-263 and G.S. ch. 125, as well as those responsibilities, powers and duties prescribed by this chapter.
