(a)

The Burke County Recreation Commission is composed of citizens of the county. One member shall be designated by the board of commissioners from the members of the board of commissioners. Other members of the commission shall represent the youth athletic playing districts. These playing districts are based on the elementary school districts in the county.

(b)

Members are not required to reside within the district they represent. However, they must have or have had a family member participating in the community program they wish to represent within the past 24 months prior to submitting application for appointment and said participation will be verified by a board of commissioners' representative.
