(a)

The county board of commissioners or their designees shall engage in a continuing program to explain and clarify the purposes and requirements of this chapter to citizens affected by it, and to guide citizens and employees in their compliance with it. The program may include publication of a brochure for affected individuals explaining the provisions of this chapter.

(b)

The county board of commissioners or their designees shall offer smoking/tobacco use cessation classes to county employees who wish to pursue this.

(c)

Employees are encouraged to talk to their health care provider about tobacco use cessation, ask about appropriate pharmacotherapy available through the county's health insurance plan and use the free quitting support services of the North Carolina Tobacco Use Quitline at 1-800-QUIT-NOW (1-800-784-8669).
