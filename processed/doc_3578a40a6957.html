(a)

It shall be unlawful for any person to consume or display in public any beer, malt, wine, spirit, liquor or other beverage which sale is regulated or licensed by the state alcoholic beverage control board or any controlled substance or any other intoxicating substance while within the boundaries of the Greenway.

(b)

It shall be unlawful for any person to be or become intoxicated by any controlled substance or any other substance while within the boundaries of the Greenway.

(c)

Any violation by any person of the foregoing sections shall be punishable as a Class 3 misdemeanor.

(d)

All definitions contained in G.S. Ch 18B apply to this section.

(Ord. of 5-6-97)
