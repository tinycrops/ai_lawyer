Each owner, operator, manager, and applicant whose name is required to be set forth on the application shall be individually responsible for compliance with each of the requirements set forth in this article and it shall not be a defense to any suspension, revocation, citation, failure to renew or other enforcement action that such person did not have knowledge of a condition, omission, failure or violation. Further, as a condition of the issuance of the license, each such person shall be deemed to have agreed to familiarize themselves with the continued operation and conditions existing on the premises at all times.
