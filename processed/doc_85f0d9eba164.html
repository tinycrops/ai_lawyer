The provisions of this article shall applicable to all new and existing manufactured home parks and any addition or expansion of existing manufactured home parks lying within the unincorporated areas of the county, but shall not be applicable to and shall not be enforced within the corporate limits or jurisdiction of any municipality unless the governing body of said municipality by resolution agrees to the enforcement of this article therein. Section 46-65 shall not apply to manufactured home parks existing at the effective date of this article, as amended on December 15, 1998.
