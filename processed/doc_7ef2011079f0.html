Any dog which appears to be lost, stray, and which is found to be not wearing a currently valid rabies vaccination tag, as required by state law or this chapter, and not under restraint in violation of this chapter, shall be impounded by the animal control department and confined in an animal shelter in a humane manner. Impoundment of such an animal shall not relieve the owner thereof from any penalty which may be imposed for violation of this chapter.
