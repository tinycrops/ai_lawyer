Notwithstanding any provisions of this division to the contrary, the board of county commissioners shall have the authority to grant nonexclusive licenses for the collection, transportation, and proper disposal of nonhazardous solid waste from commercial, industrial, and business establishments throughout the county and to grant nonexclusive licenses for the collection, transportation, and proper disposal of bulky waste, yard waste, and/or any other solid waste that is not subject to a franchise granted under division 3 of this article from any establishments throughout the county. Such licenses shall be governed by division 2 of this article.
