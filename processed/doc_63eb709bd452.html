The board of commissioners finds that the conditions causing flooding result in high and swift waters, which with debris, make the recreational, commercial or navigational use of rivers dangerous and hazardous. The board of commissioners also finds imminent flood conditions contribute to a threat of property and personal injury in those rivers areas and that, therefore, there exists a need to ban the presence of persons from waters and waterways during flood or imminent flood conditions. This article is required in furtherance of the public health, safety and welfare.
