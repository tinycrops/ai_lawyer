(a)

Required equipment in each ambulance of a franchised ambulance provider shall include, as a minimum, at all times when the ambulance is in use as such, equipment conforming to all state regulations and standards for such ambulance's designated category.

(b)

Emergency ambulance service providers ambulances shall meet category I ambulance equipment regulations and standards.

(c)

Nonemergency ambulance service providers' ambulances shall meet either category I or category II ambulance equipment regulations and standards.
