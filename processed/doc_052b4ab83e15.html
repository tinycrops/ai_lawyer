Appeal of any action taken by the planning department or the planning board under guidelines set in this article may be made by the property owner to the county planning board within ten (10) working days of written notification of required owner action, after which an appeal may be made to the board of commissioners as follows:

(1)

The property owner will notify the clerk to the board of commissioners, in writing, of the requested appeal. The clerk will then schedule the appeal at the next meeting of the board or as soon as practical thereafter, in accordance with the policies governing agenda items.

(2)

The board of commissioners shall review all information from all parties in the matter and make a determination.

(3)

In all cases, the determination of the board of commissioners shall be the final administrative review.
