Each parking space identified for the physically handicapped shall be a minimum of 12 feet, six inches wide, and at least one space shall be located and designed so as to permit easy access to the building entrance via wheelchair without egress into or crossing a public street. Additional parking spaces shall be located as near as possible to the main public entrance of a single building and centrally located in parking lots that service more than one building.
