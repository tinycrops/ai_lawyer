The initial advisory board will consist of at least two appointees for terms of one year and at least two appointees for terms of two years and at least one appointee for terms of three years. Thereafter, all appointments are to be for terms of three years, with reappointments permitted at the end of each term. No member may serve more than three consecutive full terms.

(Ord. No. 2008-16, 10-20-2008; Ord. No. 2022-26, 12-19-2022)
