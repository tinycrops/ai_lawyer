(a)

Disposal of refuse, garbage, etc.

(1)

It shall be illegal for anyone to dispose of their household or business refuse, garbage, etc., in any county garbage container.

(2)

Littering, throwing trash or waste, abandoning junked vehicles or equipment of any kind is strictly prohibited.

(b)

Pollution of waters. Except for county employees, agents and contractors on duty, a person shall not bathe animals or wash vehicles or clothing in any park area waters.
