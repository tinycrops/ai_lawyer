All new lots created on Lake James or the main stem of the Catawba River between the dams at Lake James to the mouth of Lake Rhodhiss shall be in compliance with article XII and section 1001 of the county's zoning ordinance. The reference line for parcels on the main stem of the Catawba River shall be the top edge of the river bank. All new subdivisions approved in the planned residential mixed use district after February 23, 2002, must be served by public water and sewer lines.

(Ord. of 2-20-2002; Ord. of 5-17-2005)
