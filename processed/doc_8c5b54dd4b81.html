(a)

The governing board of the Lake Norman Marine Commission shall meet at a time and place agreed upon by the boards of the four counties concerned. The commission shall meet regularly at such times and places as may be specified in its rules and regulations or in any joint resolution. Special meetings may be called as specified in the rules and regulations.

(b)

The commissioners shall elect a chairperson and such other officers as they may choose. All officers shall serve one-year terms.

(c)

The governing board shall adopt such rules and regulations as it may deem necessary, not inconsistent with this article or the provisions of any joint resolution, for the proper discharge of its duties and for the governance of the commission.

(d)

The chairperson may appoint such committees as may be authorized by such rules and regulations.
