(a)

Renewal discretionary. Upon completion of the term of any franchise granted under this chapter, the county may in its sole discretion grant or deny renewal of the franchise of the grantee in accordance with the provisions of the Cable Act. The grantee shall own the cable television system, but shall have no property right in the public rights of way upon the completion of the franchise term.
