(a)

It is the clear intent of this division to limit the materials placed into solid waste containers to solid waste generated by a residential household in the normal day-to-day course of living. The following materials are unacceptable and it shall be unlawful to place such materials in a solid waste container:

(1)

Any materials not allowed in a sanitary landfill pursuant to the state solid waste management rules of the department of human resources, division of health services, environmental health section, solid and hazardous waste management branch, April 1, 1982, as amended.

(2)

Any materials not allowed in the county sanitary landfill pursuant to the county's sanitation provisions.

(3)

Fire, embers, or hot ashes.

(4)

Automobile and truck tires.

(5)

Motor vehicles or major components thereof.

(6)

Dead animals.

(7)

Major household appliances, such as refrigerators, stoves, washers, dryers, etc.

(8)

Chairs, sofas, tables, mattresses or other items of furniture exceeding three feet at their longest dimension.

(b)

Large items that are unacceptable in solid waste containers may be taken to a permitted county sanitary landfill.
