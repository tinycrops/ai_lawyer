Equal employment opportunity. Equal opportunity employment shall be afforded by all operators of cable television systems to all qualified persons, and no person shall be discriminated against in employment because of race, color, religion, age, national origin, sex, or physical handicap. Grantee shall comply with all equal opportunity provisions enacted by federal, state and local authorities, as well as all such provisions contained in this chapter and the franchise.
