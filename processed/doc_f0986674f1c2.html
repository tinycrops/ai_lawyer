No person shall maliciously, willfully or negligently break, damage, destroy, uncover, deface or tamper with any materials, structure, appurtenance or equipment which is a part of the wastewater treatment system or belongs to WSACC, including that left upon the premises of a user discharging wastewater into the POTW. The user shall be responsible for the safety of such equipment and may be held liable in the event of any such damage.
