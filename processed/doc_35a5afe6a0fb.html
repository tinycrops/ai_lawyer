Approval of an application for issuance or renewal of a license required by this division by the sheriff shall be granted if he is satisfied as to the qualifications of the applicant, the correctness of the information contained in the application, the good moral character of the applicant or of the officers of a corporate applicant. If the license is denied because the sheriff is not so satisfied, the applicant shall be notified of the denial and the reasons therefor and, upon his request at the reasonable convenience of the board of commissioners, the applicant shall be granted an opportunity to appear before the board and to be heard with respect to his application. After such hearing, the board may grant or deny the license.
