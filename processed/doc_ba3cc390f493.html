(a)

The county board of commissioners shall appoint a director of the county emergency management agency, who shall be a person well versed and trained in planning operations involving the activities of many different agencies such as will operate to protect the public health, safety and welfare in the event of danger from enemy action or disaster.

(b)

The director shall designate and appoint deputy directors to assume the emergency duties of the director in the event of his absence or inability to act.
