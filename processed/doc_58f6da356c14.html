No earlier than 20 days from the date the assessment roll is confirmed pursuant to this article, the county tax collector shall publish a single notice that the roll has been confirmed. The notice shall state that assessments may be paid without interest at any time before the expiration of 30 days from the date the notice is published and that if they are not paid within this time all installments thereof shall bear interest as determined by the board of commissioners.
