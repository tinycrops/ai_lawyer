The director of emergency management shall review each application, review the recommendations of the review committee and upon making the following findings of fact shall issue a license or permit to the applicant:

(1)

The facility has provided all the required information and has otherwise fully complied with provisions of this article that concern the application process.

(2)

The operation of the facility is consistent with protection of human health, safety and welfare as well as the protection of the natural resources of the county.

(3)

The facility has taken or consented in writing to take any and all reasonable measures to comply with applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations.
