This chapter shall apply to every subdivision or development in the county which is located outside the planning jurisdiction of a municipality, as established pursuant to G.S. 160D-201. The power to review plans shall be treated as if it were a power authorized by G.S. 160D-201 et seq. Municipalities within the county may elect to allow this chapter to be effective within their corporate limits or their extraterritorial jurisdictions.
