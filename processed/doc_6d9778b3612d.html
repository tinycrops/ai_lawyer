(a)

All boards and commissions of the county shall be composed of members who reside within the county. Failure to meet this condition after appointment shall result in a member being deemed as resigned, and the chairperson shall immediately notify the clerk to the board of commissioners that a vacancy exists.

(b)

It shall be the duty of the chairperson of the various boards and commissions to notify the clerk to the board of commissioners should there be a resignation or any other termination of membership.

(c)

The chairperson shall be responsible for advising the clerk to the board of commissioners of changes of address of their members.

(d)

Applications for membership to all boards and commissions shall be filed with the clerk to the board of commissioners prior to appointment.

(e)

No member of any board or commission shall serve more than one unexpired term and four full terms of office consecutively, provided that this provision shall not prevent any person from serving any number of nonconsecutive terms, nor shall it apply to any board or commission whose authorization or bylaws are in conflict with this provision.
