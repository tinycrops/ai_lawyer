(a)

Any person who violates any provision of these rules and regulations shall immediately forfeit his or her right to remain in the Greenway and, upon refusal to leave the Greenway, shall considered to be trespassing.

(b)

Any person who violates any provision of these rules and regulations and ordinances shall be subject to the penalties herein provided or otherwise provided by county ordinance.

(c)

If there is any conflict between this article and any other ordinance of the county, this article shall control.

(Ord. of 5-6-97)
